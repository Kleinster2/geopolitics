# Geopolitical Media Sources

## Overview
Guide to podcasts, publications, and media sources for following geopolitical developments. Ranges from academic to accessible, hawkish to dovish. Understanding source biases and strengths helps build a balanced information diet.

## Building Information Diet

### Principles
1. **Diversify sources**: Multiple perspectives
2. **Know biases**: Understand orientation
3. **Primary sources**: Government docs, speeches
4. **Regional sources**: Not just Western
5. **Academic**: Peer-reviewed research

### Avoid
- Single source dependence
- Echo chambers
- Outrage-driven content
- Prediction-focused analysis
- Conspiracy-adjacent sources

### Quality Markers
- Transparent sourcing
- Expert authors
- Corrections policy
- Institutional backing
- Track record
