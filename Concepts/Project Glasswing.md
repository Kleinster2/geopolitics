# Project Glasswing

**Project Glasswing** is [[Anthropic]]'s restricted early-access program for its Mythos-class AI models — the frontier tier whose defensive-cyber capability (vulnerability discovery at scale) Anthropic judged too sensitive for open release. Rather than ship Mythos publicly, Anthropic gated it to a vetted consortium of roughly 150 partner organizations — reportedly including Google, Nvidia, Microsoft, and Apple, with Korean participants Samsung, SK hynix, and [[SK Telecom]] — for defensive use such as finding and patching software vulnerabilities.

The program is the institutional backdrop to the [[Anthropic model export ban (June 2026)]]: the public model, Fable 5, is the safety-gated sibling of the Glasswing-restricted Mythos 5, and the ban's reported trigger was a Glasswing partner ([[SK Telecom]]) whose suspected [[China]] ties drew US scrutiny. Glasswing is thus both the mechanism that put Mythos-class cyber capability into allied hands and the surface on which the US asserted control over who may hold it — a concrete instance of [[Weaponized Interdependence]] at the model layer ([[AI Warfare]]).
