# Axis of Resistance (Muqawama)

## Overview
Iran-led network of state and non-state actors united by opposition to Israel and US influence in the Middle East. Comprises Hezbollah, Hamas, Houthis, Iraqi militias, and Syrian government. Iran's primary tool for regional influence projection.

## Core Concept

### Arabic Term
محور المقاومة (Mihwar al-Muqawama)

### Ideological Basis
- Resistance to Israel ("Zionist entity")
- Opposition to US hegemony
- Islamic solidarity (cross-sectarian)
- Anti-imperialism
- Palestinian cause

### Strategic Purpose
- Forward defense for Iran
- Deterrence through retaliation capability
- Regional influence without direct war
- Bleeding adversaries
- Strategic depth

## Network Members

### Tier 1: Core

#### Hezbollah (Lebanon)
- **Relationship**: Oldest, deepest
- **Capability**: Most powerful non-state military
- **Role**: Northern front against Israel
- **Support**: $700M-1B/year
- **Status**: Political party + military
- See: [[Hezbollah]]

#### Syrian Government
- **Relationship**: State alliance
- **Capability**: Battered but surviving
- **Role**: Land bridge to Lebanon
- **Support**: Military, economic, advisors
- **Status**: Dependent on Iran/Russia

### Tier 2: Major Partners

#### Houthis (Ansar Allah, Yemen)
- **Relationship**: Growing since 2014
- **Capability**: Missiles, drones, naval
- **Role**: Threaten Saudi/UAE, Red Sea
- **Support**: Weapons, training, guidance
- **Status**: De facto north Yemen control
- See: [[Houthis]]

#### Iraqi Militias (PMF umbrella)
- **Relationship**: Deep, multiple groups
- **Capability**: 100,000+ fighters
- **Role**: Iraq influence, attack US bases
- **Support**: Training, weapons, funding
- **Status**: Quasi-state integration

### Tier 3: Palestinian Groups

#### Hamas
- **Relationship**: Complicated (Sunni, Qatar ties)
- **Capability**: Gaza governance, rockets
- **Role**: Palestinian resistance
- **Support**: Reduced, fluctuating
- **Status**: Pragmatic alignment

#### Palestinian Islamic Jihad (PIJ)
- **Relationship**: Closer than Hamas
- **Capability**: Smaller, more militant
- **Role**: Gaza operations
- **Support**: Significant
- **Status**: Reliable proxy
