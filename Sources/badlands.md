# badlands

## Overview

*badlands* is [[Borzou Daragahi]]'s Substack newsletter, described on its homepage as commentary and intelligence on global affairs. It is a recurring source for Middle East and adjacent geopolitical analysis, with Daragahi's comparative advantage coming from long-form foreign correspondence across Iran, Iraq, Turkey, Syria, Lebanon, North Africa, and Europe.
