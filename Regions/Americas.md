# Americas

## Overview
The Western Hemisphere, traditionally dominated by US influence (Monroe Doctrine). Latin America increasingly diversifies partnerships while grappling with governance challenges. Region relatively insulated from Eurasian conflicts but facing internal instability and great power competition.

## Sub-Regions

### North America
- United States, Canada, Mexico
- USMCA trade bloc
- Deep integration
- Security cooperation

### Central America & Caribbean
- Migration source region
- Instability drivers
- US security focus
- China diplomatic competition

### South America
- Brazil dominant
- Diverse political spectrum
- Resource wealth
- China's primary LA partner

## Key Characteristics
- **Population**: ~1 billion
- **Combined GDP**: ~$30 trillion (US dominant)
- **US sphere of influence** (historically)
- **Democratic but fragile** institutions
- **Resource rich**: oil, minerals, agriculture
