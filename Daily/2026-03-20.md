# 2026-03-20

## Key Developments
- **Day 21 of Operation Epic Fury.**
- **Land-based missiles from Gulf states (WSJ, March 18):** US fired PrSM and ATACMS from HIMARS truck launchers in the opening hours — first combat use of PrSM. ATACMS hit and sunk Iranian naval vessels and a submarine in port. Video-verified launches from Bahrain (125 miles from Iran). Iran's FM Araghchi accused US of firing at Kharg Island from UAE; UAE did not deny. No Gulf state has admitted hosting launches. 100+ Iranian naval vessels now severely damaged/destroyed/sunk (up from 90+), including 4 Soleimani-class corvettes and a drone carrier. Pentagon deliberately demonstrating same missile types planned for Pacific/Taiwan defense — signaling to China.
- **China-Iran support architecture (WSJ, March 18):** ~90% of Iran's 1.6M bbl/day crude exports go to China (~12% of China's total imports). Shadow banking network: yuan payment → front companies → Hong Kong → currency conversion. Military supply chain: Chinese companies supply Shahed drone motors, rocket fuel chemicals, weapons electronics, satellite intelligence to IRGC. In 2025, 1,000 tons of solid propellant precursor for ~260 midrange missiles shipped via IRISL. Days after Feb 28 strikes, two more IRISL ships left China — potential wartime resupply. 2023 Beijing-brokered Saudi-Iran détente now "deeply frayed."
