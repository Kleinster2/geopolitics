# Fareed Zakaria

## Overview

Foreign affairs columnist (*Washington Post*), host of CNN's *Fareed Zakaria GPS*, and contributing editor at *The Atlantic*. Centrist-internationalist commentator whose weekly synthesis tracks the median Washington/Davos foreign-policy view — useful as a signal of where establishment consensus has settled rather than as a leading indicator.

## Editorial profile

- Centrist/liberal-internationalist; consensus synthesizer rather than positional contrarian
- Reading Zakaria is reading the establishment after it has digested a development — the column reflects the settled view, not the early call
- Useful as a weather vane: when his framing shifts, the mainstream has shifted
