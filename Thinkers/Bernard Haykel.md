## Analytical Frameworks

### US-Saudi Idealism vs Realism Cycle
Frames US-Saudi relations as a recurring oscillation between idealism (human rights pressure) and realism (energy/security pragmatism). Saudi spare capacity is the structural lever that forces every American president toward engagement regardless of initial posture. See [[Mohammad bin Salman]] for full treatment.

### Political Prisoner Leverage
Assesses private diplomatic pressure as more effective than public shaming for securing prisoner releases in [[Saudi Arabia]]. Public criticism causes loss of face and hardens positions.
