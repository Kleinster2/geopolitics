# Ian Bremmer

## Overview
Political scientist, entrepreneur, and public intellectual. Founder and president of [[Eurasia Group]], the world's largest political risk consultancy. Creator of the [[G-Zero]] concept describing a world without global leadership. Prolific author, speaker, and media presence through GZERO Media.

## Key Facts
- **Born**: 1969, Boston
- **Education**: PhD Political Science, Stanford University
- **Position**: President, Eurasia Group (founded 1998)
- **Media**: Founder, GZERO Media
- **Known for**: G-Zero concept, Top Risks report

## Career

### Academic Background
- Stanford PhD in political science
- Youngest-ever national fellow at Hoover Institution
- Faculty at Columbia University
- Transitioned from academia to business

### Eurasia Group
- Founded 1998 at age 28
- Built into largest political risk firm globally
- ~200 analysts worldwide
- Clients include Fortune 500, governments, investors

### GZERO Media
- Spinoff media platform
- Daily briefings, podcasts, video
- Broader public reach than consulting
- Free access model

## Key Concepts

### [[G-Zero]]
Central intellectual contribution:
- World without global leadership
- No single power or alliance providing public goods
- "Every nation for itself"
- Post-American, but not multipolar (no alternative leader)
- First articulated ~2011

### Technopolar World
- Technology companies as geopolitical actors
- Big Tech rivaling state power
- Platform governance as quasi-sovereignty
- Digital space as contested territory

## Analytical Approach

### Perspective
- Centrist/pragmatist
- Neither hawkish nor dovish
- Skeptical of ideological frameworks
- Focus on what leaders will actually do

### Methodology
- Scenario-based thinking
- Probability weighting
- Focus on political (not just economic) risk
- Ground-level intelligence networks

## Views on Current Events (2026)

### On Trump 2.0
- Sees domestic risk as primary concern
- Predicts institutional stress
- Worried about rule of law erosion
- Notes Europe most exposed

### On Global Order
- G-Zero thesis validated
- No replacement for US leadership emerging
- Damage control mode internationally
- Long transition period ahead

## Criticism
