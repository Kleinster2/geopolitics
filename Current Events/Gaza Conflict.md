# Gaza Conflict (2023-Present)

## Overview
Major escalation of Israeli-Palestinian conflict following Hamas's October 7, 2023 attack on Israel. Israel's military response has devastated Gaza, killed tens of thousands, created humanitarian catastrophe, and sparked regional instability including Houthi attacks on shipping. Most significant Middle East crisis in decades.

## October 7 Attack

### What Happened
- Hamas-led assault from Gaza
- ~1,200 Israelis killed
- ~250 hostages taken
- Border communities, music festival targeted
- Worst attack on Jews since Holocaust

### Hamas Objectives
- Shatter normalcy
- Derail Saudi-Israel normalization
- Reassert Palestinian issue
- Provoke massive response
- Regional escalation

## Israeli Response

### Military Operation
- Stated goal: Destroy Hamas
- Ground invasion of Gaza
- Massive aerial bombardment
- Urban warfare
- Extended duration

### Casualties and Destruction
- Tens of thousands of Palestinian deaths
- Gaza infrastructure devastated
- Hospitals, schools damaged
- Mass displacement
- Humanitarian catastrophe

### Strategic Debates
- Can Hamas be "destroyed"?
- Day after governance?
- Hostage negotiations
- International pressure
- US support limits
