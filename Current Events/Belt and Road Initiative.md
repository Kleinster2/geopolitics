# Belt and Road Initiative (BRI)

## Overview
China's signature global infrastructure and investment program launched in 2013. Aims to connect Asia, Europe, and Africa through land and maritime routes. World's largest infrastructure project, reshaping global development finance and geopolitical alignments.

## Basic Facts
- **Launched**: 2013 by Xi Jinping
- **Countries**: 140+ signed MOUs
- **Investment**: $1+ trillion committed
- **Projects**: Ports, railways, roads, power plants, digital infrastructure
- **Duration**: Multi-decade vision

## Two "Silks"

### Silk Road Economic Belt (Land)
- China → Central Asia → Europe
- Rail corridors
- Energy pipelines
- Economic zones

### 21st Century Maritime Silk Road (Sea)
- South China Sea → Indian Ocean → Mediterranean
- Port network
- Shipping routes
- Coastal infrastructure

## Key Corridors

| Corridor | Route | Key Projects |
|----------|-------|--------------|
| China-Pakistan (CPEC) | Xinjiang → Gwadar | $62B, port, road, energy |
| China-Central Asia | Xinjiang → Europe | Rail, pipelines |
| China-Indochina | Yunnan → Singapore | Rail, highways |
| Bangladesh-China-India-Myanmar | Yunnan → Kolkata | Stalled |
