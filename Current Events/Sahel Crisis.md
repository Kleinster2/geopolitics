# Sahel Crisis

## Overview
Expanding security crisis across Africa's semi-arid Sahel belt. Jihadist insurgencies, military coups, governance failures, and great power competition converge. France's withdrawal and Russia's entry mark geopolitical realignment.

## Geographic Scope
- **Core affected**: Mali, Burkina Faso, Niger
- **Expanding to**: Northern Nigeria, Cameroon, Chad, Benin, Togo
- **Sahel belt**: Semi-arid transition zone south of Sahara

## Crisis Dimensions

### Security Collapse
- Jihadist territorial control
- Civilian casualties (thousands annually)
- Displacement (millions)
- State absence in rural areas
- Ethnic violence overlay

### Governance Failure
- Coups: Mali (2020, 2021), Burkina (2022), Niger (2023)
- Democratic backsliding
- Corruption
- Service delivery collapse
- Popular legitimacy crisis

### Humanitarian
- Food insecurity
- Climate stress
- Displacement camps
- Healthcare collapse
- Education disruption

- Rural control
- Tax collection ("protection")

- Ethnic manipulation

### Expansion
- Southward movement
- Coastal state threats
- Urban attacks
- Cross-border operations

## Great Power Competition

### France (Withdrawal)
- Operation Barkhane (2014-2022)
- 5,000+ troops at peak
- Counter-terrorism focus
- Anti-French sentiment growth
- Withdrawal (Mali 2022, Niger 2023)

### Russia/Wagner Group
- Mali deployment (2021)
- Burkina Faso ties
- Niger interest
- Mining concessions
- Human rights abuses
- Anti-French narrative

### United States
- Limited presence
- Niger drone base (expelled 2024)
- ISR assets
- Training programs
- Reassessing approach

### China
- Economic interests
- Infrastructure investment
- Non-intervention stance
- Long-term engagement

## Coup Wave

### Mali (2020, 2021)
- Colonel Goïta seizure
- Wagner invitation
- France expelled
- ECOWAS sanctions (lifted)

### Burkina Faso (2022)
- Captain Traoré takeover
- France expelled
- Russia embrace
- Continued insecurity

### Niger (2023)
- General Tchiani coup
- France/US expelled
- ECOWAS intervention threat
- Alliance with Mali/Burkina

### Alliance of Sahel States
- Mali, Burkina Faso, Niger
- Mutual defense pact
- ECOWAS withdrawal
- Anti-Western orientation
