# Norway

## Overview
Wealthy Nordic kingdom and NATO founding member. Controls critical Arctic territory including Svalbard. Major energy exporter (oil, gas) that became Europe's largest gas supplier after Russia cut flows. Home to Kongsberg defense company. Strategic Arctic and North Atlantic position.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Oslo
- **Population**: 5.5 million
- **GDP**: ~$580 billion (one of world's highest per capita)
- **Government**: Constitutional monarchy
