# Philippines

## Overview
US treaty ally and frontline state in South China Sea disputes with China. Strategically located at the first island chain, now deepening security ties with Washington after years of tension. Marcos Jr. administration marks sharp pivot toward US, contrasting with predecessor Duterte's China tilt.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Manila
- **Population**: ~115 million
- **GDP**: ~$405 billion
- **President**: Ferdinand Marcos Jr. (2022-)
- **Alliance**: Mutual Defense Treaty with US (1951)
- **Key Location**: Luzon Strait, Bashi Channel
