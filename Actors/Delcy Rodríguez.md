# Delcy Rodríguez

## Overview
Acting President of Venezuela since January 3, 2026, following US capture of Nicolás Maduro in Operation Absolute Resolve. Previously Executive Vice President and key Maduro loyalist. Sanctioned by US and EU. Maintains regime continuity despite Maduro's removal.

## Key Facts
- **Position**: Acting President of Venezuela (since January 3, 2026)
- **Previous**: Executive Vice President (2017-2026)
- **Party**: PSUV (United Socialist Party)
- **Sanctions**: US, EU, Canada, Switzerland

## Background
- Sister of Jorge Rodríguez (National Assembly President)
- Former Foreign Minister (2014-2017)
- Former Communications Minister
- Lawyer by training

## Post-Maduro Role

### Maintaining Regime
Following Maduro's capture:
- Declared "There is only one president - Nicolás Maduro"
- Kept interior minister and defense minister in place
- Security services continue operating
- Released 68 political prisoners (of 800+ held)

### Challenges
- US: "No American is safe in Venezuela"
- Regime reestablishing terror networks
- Trump: "US is in charge of Venezuela"
- International isolation continues

## US Assessment
State Department warning that Maduro's security apparatus remains intact under Rodríguez. Terror networks that kept Maduro in power remain operational.

## Sanctions
Under US, EU, and other sanctions for:
- Human rights violations
- Undermining democracy
- Corruption
