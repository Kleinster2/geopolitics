# JD Vance

## Overview
50th Vice President of the United States (2025-). Iraq War veteran who returned a sharp critic of foreign military interventions. Senator from Ohio (2023-2025). Author of *Hillbilly Elegy* (2016). Core figure in "America First" populist-nationalist wing of Republican Party.

## Background
- **Born**: 1984, Middletown, Ohio
- **Military**: US Marine Corps, Iraq War veteran
- **Education**: Ohio State, Yale Law School
- **Career**: Venture capital, author, Senator (Ohio 2023-2025)
