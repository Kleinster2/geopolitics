# Javier Milei

## Overview
President of Argentina (2023-present). Libertarian economist who won on radical reform platform. Strong alignment with Trump and Israel. Confirmed founding member of the [[Board of Peace]].

## Positions

### Domestic (On-the-Record)
- Radical fiscal austerity ("chainsaw" approach)
- Attempted dollarization of economy
- Deregulation and state reduction
- Eliminated multiple ministries

### Foreign Policy (On-the-Record)
- Strong US alignment (particularly Trump)
- Pro-Israel stance (visited Western Wall)
- Anti-China, anti-leftist Latin American governments
- Withdrew Argentina from BRICS membership process
- Break from predecessor's non-aligned posture

### Board of Peace Response
Confirmed founding member - among most enthusiastic early supporters. Consistent with strong Trump alignment.
