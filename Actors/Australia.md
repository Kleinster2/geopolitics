# Australia

## Overview
US treaty ally and Five Eyes member navigating between security dependence on America and economic dependence on China. AUKUS partnership marks major strategic bet. Resource-rich middle power increasingly engaged in Indo-Pacific security.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Canberra
- **Population**: ~26 million
- **GDP**: ~$1.7 trillion
- **Military Budget**: ~$35 billion (growing to 2%+ GDP)
- **Monarch**: King Charles III (ceremonial)
- **Prime Minister**: Head of government

## Geopolitical Position

### Strategic Geography
- Continent-sized island
- Distance from threats
- Indo-Pacific gateway
- Resource abundance
- Southern Hemisphere leader

### The Australia Dilemma
```
Security → United States (ally)
         ↕ TENSION
Economy → China (largest trade partner)
```
