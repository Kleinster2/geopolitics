# Benjamin Netanyahu

## Overview
Prime Minister of Israel (2022-present; previously 1996-1999, 2009-2021). Longest-serving Israeli PM. Central figure in [[Gaza Conflict]], [[Iran-Israel Shadow War]], and [[Board of Peace]] dynamics.

## Positions

### Current Term (2022-present)
- Leads coalition with far-right and ultra-Orthodox parties
- Oversaw military response to October 7, 2023 Hamas attack
- Facing domestic protests over judicial reform and war management

### Core Policy Positions (On-the-Record)
- Iran as existential threat to Israel
- Opposition to Palestinian statehood
- Expansion of Abraham Accords normalization
- Close US-Israel alliance as strategic necessity

## Board of Peace Response
Initially objected: makeup of Gaza committee "not coordinated with Israel and runs contrary to its policy" (concern over Qatar and Turkey inclusion). Israel subsequently joined as signatory.
