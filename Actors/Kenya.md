# Kenya

## Overview
East Africa's economic hub and most stable democracy in a turbulent region. Key US counterterrorism partner. Gateway to East African markets. Hosts major refugee populations. Balances Western ties with growing China engagement. Regional leader managing complex neighborhood including Somalia, Ethiopia, and South Sudan.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Nairobi
- **Population**: ~55 million
- **GDP**: ~$115 billion
- **President**: William Ruto (2022-)
- **Former Colonial Power**: United Kingdom
- **Independence**: 1963
