# Xiaomi

Chinese consumer-electronics and EV maker. Geopolitically notable for successfully challenging its designation under [[Executive Order 13959]]: added to the Pentagon's "Communist Chinese Military Companies" list in January 2021, Xiaomi sued in US federal court, won a preliminary injunction (the court found the designation arbitrary and capricious), and was removed from the restrictions in 2021 — a rare due-process check on US capital-markets sanctions.
