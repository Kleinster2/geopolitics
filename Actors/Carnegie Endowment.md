# Carnegie Endowment for International Peace

## Overview
One of America's oldest foreign policy think tanks, distinguished by its global network of centers. Founded by Andrew Carnegie to promote international peace. Known for deep regional expertise, particularly on Russia, China, Middle East, and nuclear issues. Liberal internationalist orientation with strong academic reputation.
