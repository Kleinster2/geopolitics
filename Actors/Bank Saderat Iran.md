# Bank Saderat Iran

## Overview
Major Iranian state-owned bank specializing in export financing. Heavily sanctioned by US for alleged support of terrorism, particularly Hezbollah financing.

## Key Facts
- **Persian name**: بانک صادرات ایران
- **Meaning**: "Export Bank of Iran"
- **Established**: 1952
- **Type**: State-owned commercial bank
- **Specialization**: Export/trade finance
- **Status**: Operating, heavily sanctioned

## Sanctions
Designated by US Treasury specifically for allegedly facilitating financial services for Hezbollah and other designated entities. Subject to some of the strictest sanctions among Iranian banks.

## Allegations
US government has alleged Bank Saderat:
- Transferred funds to [[Hezbollah]]
- Facilitated transactions for designated terrorists
- Supported Iran's proliferation activities

Bank and Iranian government deny allegations.

## Current Status (2026)
Operating domestically but severely constrained internationally. Part of the broader [[Iranian Banking System]] crisis.
