# European Council on Foreign Relations (ECFR)

## Overview
Pan-European foreign policy think tank founded to promote a more unified European voice in international affairs. Network-based model with offices across Europe. Led EU foreign policy thinking on issues from Russia to China to strategic autonomy. Influential in shaping European debate during period of EU's growing geopolitical ambition.

## Key Facts
- **Founded**: 2007
- **Headquarters**: Berlin (+ offices across Europe)
- **Director**: Mark Leonard (co-founder)
- **Model**: Pan-European network
- **Focus**: EU foreign policy
