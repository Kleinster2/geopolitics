# Qasem Soleimani

## Overview
Legendary commander of [[Quds Force]] (1998-2020), architect of Iran's regional proxy network, and the second most powerful figure in Iran after the Supreme Leader. His assassination by US drone strike in January 2020 marked a dramatic escalation in US-Iran tensions.

## Biography

### Early Life
- **Born**: March 11, 1957, Qanat-e Malek, Iran
- **Background**: Poor rural family
- **Education**: Limited formal education
- **Work**: Construction laborer before revolution

### Revolutionary Period
- Joined Revolutionary Guards (1979)
- Self-taught military skills
- Rose through ranks rapidly
- Iran-Iraq War veteran

### Iran-Iraq War (1980-1988)
- Combat commander
- 41st Tharallah Division
- Reputation for bravery
- Kurdish front operations

### Quds Force Command (1998-2020)
- Appointed by Khamenei
- Transformed external operations
- Built proxy network
- Regional kingmaker
