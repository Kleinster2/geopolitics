# SK Telecom

**SK Telecom** is South Korea's largest mobile carrier and a unit of the SK Group conglomerate. It became a geopolitically significant actor in June 2026 as the reported trigger for the [[Anthropic model export ban (June 2026)]]: as an Anthropic investor and a [[Project Glasswing]] partner, it had been granted early access to the restricted Claude Mythos model, and US officials' concern over the carrier's suspected [[China]] ties reportedly prompted Washington to order Anthropic to revoke that access — days before the blanket foreign-national export directive that took Mythos 5 and Fable 5 offline worldwide.

## The China-ties question

US officials raised concerns about SK Telecom's historical business links to China. The basis is thin and contested:

- Parent SK Group held a stake in China Unicom until 2009. (reported)
- SK Telecom reported only about $1.9M of China revenue and roughly seven staff in China in 2024. (reported)
- SK Telecom denies any ties to China; no underlying evidence has been made public. (disputed)

The episode reportedly put South Korea's whole telecom sector on alert, and illustrates the model-layer logic of the ban: the stated concern attached not to the ally as such but to an adversary-exposed firm operating inside it — a concrete instance of [[Weaponized Interdependence]].
