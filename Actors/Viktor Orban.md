# Viktor Orban

## Overview
Prime Minister of Hungary (2010-present; previously 1998-2002). Leading figure in European illiberal politics. Closest EU leader to both Trump and Putin. Frequent outlier within NATO and EU on Russia sanctions and Ukraine support.

## Positions

### Foreign Policy (On-the-Record)
- Opposed EU sanctions on Russia
- Blocked or delayed EU Ukraine aid packages
- Close personal relationship with Putin (continued visits during war)
- Strong alignment with Trump on immigration, "sovereignty"
- Self-described "illiberal democracy" model

### Board of Peace Response
Called invitation an "honor" - among most enthusiastic supporters. Consistent with alignment to Trump and willingness to engage Russia.

### EU/NATO Friction
- Repeatedly vetoed or delayed EU consensus on Ukraine
- Blocked Sweden's NATO accession (delayed until 2024)
- "Peacekeeping mission" visit to Moscow (2024) without EU mandate
- Hungary held EU Council presidency (H2 2024) amid controversy
