# Mark Carney

## Overview
Prime Minister of Canada (2025-present). Former Governor of the Bank of England (2013-2020) and Bank of Canada (2008-2013). Economist navigating Canada-US relations under Trump 2.0 tariff pressure.

## Positions

### Background
- **Governor, Bank of England** (2013-2020) - First non-British holder in modern era
- **Governor, Bank of Canada** (2008-2013) - Steered through financial crisis
- **UN Special Envoy for Climate Action and Finance** (2019-2023)

### Board of Peace Response
Accepted "in principle" but stated Canada won't pay the $1 billion fee; terms need discussion. Trump revoked Canada's invitation on January 23, 2026, following Carney's conditional response.

### US-Canada Relations
- Managing Trump tariff threats under [[Trade War 2025-2026]]
- Balancing sovereignty with economic dependence on US
