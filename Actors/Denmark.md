# Denmark

## Overview
Nordic constitutional monarchy and NATO founding member. Controls Greenland and Faroe Islands as part of the Danish Realm. Now at center of unprecedented crisis as fellow NATO ally (United States) threatens to forcibly annex its territory.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Copenhagen
- **Population**: 5.9 million
- **GDP**: ~$400 billion
- **Government**: Constitutional monarchy
- **Monarch**: King Frederik X (2024-)
- **Prime Minister**: Mette Frederiksen (2019-)
- **NATO Member**: Founding member (1949)
- **EU Member**: Since 1973

## The Danish Realm
Denmark proper plus two autonomous territories:

| Territory | Population | Status |
|-----------|------------|--------|
| **Denmark** | 5.9 million | Core state |
| **Greenland** | 57,000 | Autonomous since 2009 |
| **Faroe Islands** | 54,000 | Self-governing since 1948 |
