# Hakan Fidan

## Overview
Foreign Minister of Turkey (2023-present). Former head of Turkey's National Intelligence Organization (MIT, 2010-2023). Key figure in Turkish regional diplomacy. Member of the [[Board of Peace]] Gaza Executive Board (GEB).

## Positions

### Current
- **Foreign Minister of Turkey** (2023-present)
- Member, Board of Peace Gaza Executive Board

### Previous
- **Director, MIT (National Intelligence Organization)** (2010-2023)
- Longest-serving MIT chief in modern Turkish history
- Oversaw Turkey's intelligence operations in Syria, Iraq, Libya

## Background
Former intelligence chief turned diplomat. Close to [[Recep Tayyip Erdogan]]. His appointment as Foreign Minister in 2023 signaled Turkey's assertive regional posture. Known for back-channel diplomacy and operational experience.

## Board of Peace Role
Named to the Gaza Executive Board, representing Turkey's interest in Palestinian issues and Gaza reconstruction. Netanyahu cited Turkish and Qatari officials on the Gaza committee as a concern.
