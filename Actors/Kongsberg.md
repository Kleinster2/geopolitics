# Kongsberg (Kongsberg Gruppen)

## Overview
Norway's leading defense and aerospace company. Best known for NASAMS air defense system used worldwide. Key partner in US-Norwegian defense cooperation. Producer of naval strike missiles and remote weapon stations. Punching above weight for small country.

## Key Facts
- **Headquarters**: Kongsberg, Norway
- **Founded**: 1814 (as state weapons factory)
- **Employees**: ~12,000
- **Revenue**: ~$4 billion
- **Ownership**: Norwegian government (50.004%)

## Core Products

### Air Defense
- **NASAMS** - Medium-range air defense system
  - Co-developed with Raytheon
  - Uses AMRAAM missiles
  - Deployed by 12+ countries including US
  - Critical Ukraine war system
  - Part of NATO drone wall concept

### Naval Systems
- **NSM (Naval Strike Missile)** - Anti-ship/land attack
- **JSM (Joint Strike Missile)** - F-35 compatible version
- Naval combat systems
- Autonomous underwater vehicles

### Remote Weapon Systems
- **CROWS** - US military's primary remote weapon station
- **Protector** series - Vehicle-mounted weapons
- Used on thousands of US and allied vehicles

### Space & Surveillance
- Satellite ground systems
- Maritime surveillance
- Space technology

## Strategic Significance

### NASAMS Success
- Protects Washington DC (only foreign system to do so)
- Major Ukraine war performer
- Cheaper than Patriot, effective vs drones/missiles
- Key to European air defense architecture

### US Partnership Model
- Deep integration with Raytheon on NASAMS
- JSM for F-35 program
- Shows how small ally can be major contributor
- Norwegian-American defense synergy

### Ukraine War Role
- NASAMS systems to Ukraine (highly effective)
- NSM missiles for coastal defense
- Expanded production for NATO

## Norway's Defense Niche
- High-tech systems from small country
- Government ownership ensures sovereignty
- Arctic/maritime specialization
- NATO's northern flank expertise
