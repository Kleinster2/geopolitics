# Robert Gabriel Jr.

## Overview
American political advisor, speechwriter, and former television producer. Deputy National Security Advisor (May 2025-present). Assistant to the President for Policy (January 2025-present). Member of the [[Board of Peace]] founding executive board.

## Positions

### Trump 2.0
- **Deputy National Security Advisor** (May 2025-present) - Named alongside Andy Baker as part of [[Marco Rubio]]'s NSC restructuring
- **Assistant to the President for Policy** (January 2025-present)
- Member, Board of Peace founding executive board (January 2026)

### Previous
- **Associate Producer**, *The Ingraham Angle* (Fox News)
- **Special Assistant**, Stephen Miller's staff (January 2017)
- **Policy Advisor**, Trump 2016 presidential campaign

## Background
Unusual trajectory from campaign policy staff to TV production to senior NSC role. His appointment as Deputy NSA was part of Rubio's restructuring of the National Security Council in his capacity as acting national security advisor.
