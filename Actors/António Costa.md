# António Costa

## Overview
President of the [[European Council]] since December 2024. Former Prime Minister of Portugal (2015-2024). Represents EU at head-of-state level alongside [[European Commission]] President.

## Key Facts
- **Born**: 1961, Lisbon
- **Party**: PS (Portuguese Socialist Party), PES (European)
- **European Council President**: December 2024-present
- **Previous role**: Prime Minister of Portugal (2015-2024)

## European Council Role

### The Position
- Chairs European Council meetings (EU heads of state/government)
- Represents EU externally at summit level
- Sets strategic agenda (with Commission)
- 2.5-year term, renewable once

### Distinction from Commission President
| Role | Focus | Represents |
|------|-------|------------|
| European Council President | Strategic direction, summits | Member state leaders |
| Commission President | Executive action, legislation | EU as institution |

Both travel together for major diplomatic events (e.g., India visit January 2026).

Managing EU response to:

## Portuguese Politics Background

### Prime Minister (2015-2024)
- Led Socialist minority government with left-wing support ("geringonça")
- Won outright majority in 2022
- Resigned November 2023 amid corruption investigation (later cleared)
- Known for fiscal discipline despite left-wing coalition

### Political Style
- Pragmatic center-left
- Coalition builder
- Pro-European integration
