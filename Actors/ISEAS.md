# ISEAS-Yusof Ishak Institute

## Overview
Singapore's premier Southeast Asia research institute. Authoritative source on ASEAN politics, economics, and regional affairs. Known for annual "State of Southeast Asia" survey capturing elite regional opinion. Bridge between academic research and policy relevance. Essential resource for understanding Southeast Asian perspectives.

## Key Facts
- **Founded**: 1968
- **Headquarters**: Singapore
- **Named For**: Yusof Ishak (Singapore's first president)
- **Affiliation**: Autonomous institution under NTU
- **Focus**: Southeast Asia
