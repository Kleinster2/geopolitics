# Jared Kushner

## Overview
Trump's son-in-law and senior advisor. Architect of the Abraham Accords (2020). Member of the [[Board of Peace]] founding executive board. Runs Affinity Partners, a private equity firm with significant Gulf state investment.

## Positions

### Trump 2.0
- Member, Board of Peace founding executive board (January 2026)
- No formal White House title in second term

### Trump 1.0
- **Senior Advisor to the President** (2017-2021)
- Led Abraham Accords negotiations (Israel-UAE, Israel-Bahrain, Israel-Morocco normalization)
- Developed "Peace to Prosperity" plan for Israeli-Palestinian conflict

### Post-Government
- **Affinity Partners** - Private equity firm launched 2021
- Received $2 billion from Saudi Public Investment Fund (reported)

## Relevance
Kushner's inclusion on the Board of Peace executive board connects his Abraham Accords experience and Gulf relationships to the new body's mandate.
