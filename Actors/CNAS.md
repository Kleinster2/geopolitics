# Center for a New American Security (CNAS)

## Overview
Washington national security think tank known as pipeline for Democratic administration officials. Founded by Kurt Campbell and Michèle Flournoy. Strong focus on Asia, defense policy, and technology. Centrist-hawkish orientation. Major source of Biden administration national security staff.

## Key Facts
- **Founded**: 2007
- **Headquarters**: Washington, DC
- **CEO**: Richard Fontaine
- **Founders**: Kurt Campbell, Michèle Flournoy
- **Focus**: National security, defense
