# Atlantic Council

## Overview
Washington-based think tank promoting transatlantic cooperation and US global leadership. Strong focus on NATO, European security, and increasingly on great power competition with China and Russia. Known for aggressive digital presence and rapid-response analysis. More hawkish orientation than some peers.

## Key Facts
- **Founded**: 1961
- **Headquarters**: Washington, DC
- **President/CEO**: Frederick Kempe
- **Focus**: Transatlantic, global security
- **Style**: Rapid, media-savvy
