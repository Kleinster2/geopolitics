# Chris Ciauri

**Chris Ciauri** is Anthropic's Managing Director of International, responsible for the company's commercial expansion outside the United States. He surfaces in the geopolitics vault as Anthropic's public voice during the [[Anthropic model export ban (June 2026)]]: at a June 17, 2026 press conference in Seoul — held to mark Anthropic's Korean office expansion even as the export ban cut Korean access to its top models — he said the company was *"very confident that in the coming days, the models will become available again,"* without naming a date or conditions.
