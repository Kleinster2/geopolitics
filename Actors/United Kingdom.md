# United Kingdom

## Overview
Former global hegemon maintaining outsized influence through permanent UNSC seat, nuclear deterrent, Five Eyes membership, and financial center. Post-Brexit, seeks "Global Britain" role while managing diminished European ties.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: London
- **Population**: ~67 million
- **GDP**: ~$3.1 trillion (6th largest)
- **Military Budget**: ~$65 billion
- **Nuclear Warheads**: ~225 (Trident submarines)
- **Monarch**: Ceremonial head of state
- **Prime Minister**: Head of government
