# Germany

## Overview
Europe's largest economy and the EU's de facto leader. Historically restrained on security, now undergoing "Zeitenwende" (turning point) in response to Russia's invasion of Ukraine. Balances Atlanticism with European autonomy.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Berlin
- **Population**: ~84 million
- **GDP**: ~$4.5 trillion (4th largest)
- **Military Budget**: ~$65 billion (rising to 2% GDP)
- **Chancellor**: Head of government
- **EU Role**: Largest member, Franco-German engine

## Geopolitical Position

### Historical Constraints
- WWII legacy → military restraint
- Pacifist political culture
- Export-oriented economy
- Russia energy dependence
- "Checkbook diplomacy"

### Zeitenwende (Feb 2022)
Chancellor Scholz announced historic shift:
- €100 billion defense fund
- 2% GDP defense spending
- Weapons to Ukraine
- Energy diversification
- Strategic reorientation

## Strategic Priorities
1. European integration/stability
2. Transatlantic alliance
3. Russia deterrence
4. Economic competitiveness
5. Energy transition
6. China policy recalibration

## Key Relationships

### Core Partners
- [[France]] - EU engine, occasional tensions
- [[United States]] - Security guarantor
- [[European Union]] - Central role
- [[NATO]] - Recommitted member

### Complex
- [[Russia]] - Ruptured (post-2022)
- [[China]] - Economic ties vs. systemic rivalry

## Economic Profile
- Export champion (autos, machinery, chemicals)
- Manufacturing powerhouse
- Energy-intensive industry
- China market dependence
- Competitiveness concerns

## Energy Transformation
### Pre-2022
- Nord Stream 1 & 2 (Russia gas)
- Nuclear phase-out
- Coal reduction
- Renewables expansion

### Post-2022
- Russia gas cut-off
- LNG terminals (rapid build)
- Coal temporary extension
- Renewables acceleration
- Industrial stress

## Defense Posture
- Bundeswehr modernization
- F-35 purchase (nuclear sharing)
- Air defense (European Sky Shield)
- Troop presence in Baltics/Poland
- Capability gaps persist

## Domestic Politics

- Immigration debates

## Challenges
- Industrial competitiveness
- Energy costs
- Defense execution
