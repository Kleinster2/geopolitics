# Quds Force

## Overview
The extraterritorial arm of [[IRGC]], responsible for external operations, proxy management, and unconventional warfare. Iran's primary instrument for projecting power and influence across the Middle East without direct military involvement.

## Basic Facts
- **Full Name**: Niru-ye Qods (Jerusalem Force)
- **Part of**: IRGC
- **Personnel**: ~5,000-15,000 (estimates)
- **Commander**: Brigadier General Esmail Qaani
- **Previous Commander**: Major General Qasem Soleimani (killed 2020)

## Mission
1. Export Islamic Revolution
2. Support "resistance" movements
3. Manage proxy relationships
4. Conduct special operations
5. Gather intelligence
6. Counter US/Israeli influence

## Organizational Structure

### Directorates
- **Operations**: Combat planning, proxy coordination
- **Intelligence**: Regional networks, targeting
- **Finance**: Funding channels, sanctions evasion
- **Logistics**: Arms transfers, supply chains
