# China

## Overview
The world's most populous country and second-largest economy, pursuing "national rejuvenation" under CCP leadership. Primary strategic competitor to US-led order, with growing global economic and military reach.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Beijing
- **Population**: ~1.4 billion
- **GDP**: ~$18 trillion (PPP: ~$30 trillion)
- **Military Budget**: ~$225 billion (official; likely higher)
- **Nuclear Warheads**: ~500 (rapidly expanding)
- **Leader**: Xi Jinping (General Secretary since 2012)

## Geopolitical Position

### Strategic Goals
1. **Taiwan unification**: Core interest, potential flashpoint
2. **Regional primacy**: Dominant position in Asia-Pacific
3. **Belt and Road Initiative**: Global infrastructure/influence
4. **Technology self-sufficiency**: Escape US dependency
5. **Military modernization**: World-class force by 2049

### Core Interests (Non-Negotiable)
- Taiwan
- South China Sea claims
- Tibet, Xinjiang sovereignty
- CCP rule

## Foreign Policy Concepts
- **Wolf Warrior Diplomacy**: Assertive, nationalist posture
- **Dual Circulation**: Domestic + international economic strategy
- **Community of Shared Future**: Alternative to Western order

## Territorial Disputes
- **Taiwan**: Claims full sovereignty
- **South China Sea**: Nine-dash line claims
- **East China Sea**: Senkaku/Diaoyu with [[Japan]]
- **India**: LAC border disputes

## Key Relationships

### Partners
- [[Russia]] (strategic partnership)
- [[Pakistan]] (all-weather friendship)
- [[North Korea]] (complicated ally)
- Global South outreach via BRI

### Rivals
- [[United States]] (strategic competition)
- [[Japan]] (historical tensions)
- [[India]] (border disputes)
- [[Australia]] (deteriorated relations)

## Strengths
- Manufacturing dominance
- Population scale
- State capacity for long-term planning
- Technology advancement
- Growing military capabilities

## Vulnerabilities
- Demographics (aging population)
- Energy import dependence
- Taiwan scenario costs
- Technology chokepoints (semiconductors)
- Real estate/debt issues
