# Ajay Banga

## Overview
President of the World Bank (2023-present). Former CEO of Mastercard (2010-2021). Indian-American business executive. Member of the [[Board of Peace]] founding executive board.

## Positions

### Current
- **World Bank President** (2023-present)
- Member, Board of Peace founding executive board (January 2026)

### Previous

- **President and COO, Citigroup Asia Pacific** (2008-2009)

Banga's inclusion on the Board of Peace executive board suggests a development finance dimension to the body's mandate, potentially connecting conflict resolution to reconstruction funding.
