# Forward Global

## Overview

Forward Global is an international risk-management and strategic-advisory group headquartered in Paris, with offices across Europe, North America, and Africa. The firm describes its mission as helping executives, companies, and institutions understand, prepare for, and manage digital, economic, information, reputational, political, regulatory, cyber, and emerging-technology risks.

For the vault, Forward Global matters less as a policy actor than as a private-sector interpretation node: its consultants, including [[Paymon Azmoudeh]], translate geopolitical shocks into risk, compliance, crisis-management, and investment-intelligence questions for clients.

## Key Facts

- Headquarters: Paris
- Offices listed by Forward Global: Paris, Washington, Brussels, London, Montreal, New York, Abidjan, and Miami
- Services: cybersecurity; litigation, arbitration, and dispute support; combating illicit trade; investment strategy and intelligence; M&A intelligence; risk and compliance investigations; strategic communications and government affairs
- Technology platforms listed by the firm: Leakid, Ambionics, Uncovery, Dataleaks, Evanesco, Ubik Learning Academy, Dilitrack, and Lexhunt
- Growth path: Forward Global's presentation describes a roll-up model across public affairs, cybersecurity, investigations, strategic communications, and MENA/Africa risk analysis, including the 2020 acquisition of l'Observatoire des Pays Arabes and the 2022 acquisition of Forward Risk

## Analytical Position

Forward Global's public-facing frame is practitioner-oriented rather than academic: geopolitics is treated as a source of risk to be mapped, mitigated, communicated, investigated, or converted into strategic positioning. This makes the firm relevant when tracking how conflicts and regulatory changes affect companies, investors, institutions, and crisis managers.

The firm should be grouped with private political-risk and strategic-advisory actors such as [[Eurasia Group]], [[Stratfor]], and [[RANE]], while noting that Forward Global has a stronger cyber/investigations/strategic-communications stack than a pure forecasting firm.

## Caveats

- Forward Global is a client-service firm. Public commentary by its consultants may reflect a risk-management lens rather than a full geopolitical theory.
- Because its work includes strategic communications and government affairs, treat firm-produced framing as interested analysis unless independently corroborated.
- Services span many domains; avoid assuming a single house view across all consultants.
