# Myanmar (Burma)

## Overview
Southeast Asian nation in crisis following 2021 military coup that reversed democratic transition. Now engulfed in civil war with military regime (Tatmadaw) fighting resistance forces across the country. Strategic location between India and China. ASEAN's most acute challenge. Humanitarian catastrophe with regime losing territorial control.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Naypyidaw
- **Population**: ~55 million
- **GDP**: ~$65 billion (collapsed)
- **Regime**: Military junta (SAC)
- **Deposed Leader**: Aung San Suu Kyi (imprisoned)
- **Status**: Civil war

## 2021 Coup

### What Happened
- February 1, 2021
- Military seized power
- Election fraud pretext
- Aung San Suu Kyi detained
- Democratic transition ended

### Aftermath
- Mass protests
- Violent crackdown
- Civil disobedience movement
- Armed resistance emerged
- International condemnation
