# Sigrid Kaag

## Overview
Former Deputy Prime Minister and Finance Minister of the Netherlands. Former UN Senior Coordinator for the OPCW-UN Joint Mission in Syria (2014-2015). Member of the [[Board of Peace]] Gaza Executive Board (GEB).

## Positions

### Current
- Member, Board of Peace Gaza Executive Board

### Previous
- **Deputy Prime Minister and Finance Minister, Netherlands** (2022-2023)
- **Minister of Foreign Trade and Development Cooperation** (2017-2021)
- **Minister of Finance** (2021-2022)
- **UN Senior Coordinator**, OPCW-UN Joint Mission in Syria (2014-2015)
- **UNRWA Deputy Commissioner-General**
- Extensive career in UN and humanitarian organizations across the Middle East

## Board of Peace Role
Brings UN and Middle East humanitarian expertise to the Gaza Executive Board. Her background in Syrian chemical weapons dismantlement and UNRWA gives operational knowledge of the region. Represents European/Dutch involvement despite the Netherlands not being a Board of Peace signatory.
