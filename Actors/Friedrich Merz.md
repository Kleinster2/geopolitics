# Friedrich Merz

## Overview
German Chancellor since 2025 (CDU/CSU). Led center-right return to power after Scholz government collapsed. Facing pressure to increase defense spending and take stronger European leadership role as US retreats from security commitments.

## Key Facts
- **Position**: Chancellor of Germany
- **Party**: Christian Democratic Union (CDU)
- **In Office**: 2025-present
- **Previous**: Corporate lawyer, BlackRock Germany chairman

## Policy Positions

### Defense & Security
- Supports increased military spending
- Open to German troop deployments (January 2026): "not ruling anything out" regarding Ukraine peacekeeping
- Committed to NATO but navigating US pressure for higher spending

### European Leadership
- Taking more prominent role as Macron weakened domestically
- Franco-German relationship strained but functional
- Key figure in European response to Trump administration

### Ukraine
- Supports continued aid to Ukraine
- At Paris "Coalition of the Willing" summit (January 2026)
- Could deploy troops to neighboring NATO territory; considering Ukraine deployment after ceasefire

## January 2026

At Paris summit on Ukraine security guarantees:
- Attended alongside Macron, Starmer, and 32 other nations
- Germany "not ruling anything out" on troop deployments
- More cautious than France/UK but engaged
