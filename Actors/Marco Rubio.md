# Marco Rubio

## Overview
US Secretary of State in Trump 2.0 (confirmed 2025). Former US Senator from Florida (2011-2025). Known for hawkish positions on China and Latin America. Member of the [[Board of Peace]] founding executive board.

## Positions

### Trump 2.0
- **Secretary of State** (2025-present)
- Member, Board of Peace founding executive board

### Previous
- **US Senator, Florida** (2011-2025)
- Senate Foreign Relations Committee
- Senate Intelligence Committee

## Policy Positions (On-the-Record)

### China
- Long-standing China hawk
- Advocated sanctions on Chinese officials over Hong Kong, Xinjiang
- Supported Taiwan defense measures

### Latin America
- Hard line on Venezuela, Cuba, Nicaragua
- Supported maximum pressure on Maduro regime

## Board of Peace Role
Named to the founding executive board alongside Witkoff, Kushner, Blair, and Banga.
