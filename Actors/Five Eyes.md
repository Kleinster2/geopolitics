# Five Eyes

## Overview
Intelligence-sharing alliance between the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, and New Zealand. Deepest intelligence partnership in the world, originating from WWII cooperation. Now central to Western intelligence on China, Russia, and global threats. Anglosphere core with shared language, legal traditions, and strategic alignment.

## Member States
1. **United States** - NSA, CIA
2. **United Kingdom** - GCHQ, MI6
3. **United States** - NSA, CIA
4. **Canada** - CSE, CSIS
5. **Australia** - ASD, ASIS
6. **New Zealand** - GCSB, NZSIS
