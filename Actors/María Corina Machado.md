# María Corina Machado

## Overview
Venezuelan opposition leader and Nobel Peace Prize laureate. Led the democratic opposition against the Maduro regime, gaining international recognition for her persistence despite being barred from running for office. After Operation Absolute Resolve removed Maduro, she emerged as a key figure in discussions of Venezuela's political future.

## Key Facts
- **Born**: 1967
- **Position**: Opposition leader, founder of Vente Venezuela
- **Nobel Peace Prize**: Awarded (year TBD) for pro-democracy activism
- **Status**: Barred from holding office by Maduro regime

## Political Career

### Opposition Leadership
- Founded Vente Venezuela (liberal political party)
- Consistently advocated for democratic transition
- Survived regime persecution, arrest warrants
- Maintained opposition coalition despite repression

### 2024 Election
- Barred from running by regime
- Supported Edmundo González as candidate
- Opposition claimed victory based on vote tallies
- Regime declared Maduro winner despite evidence

### Post-Intervention Role
Following US capture of Maduro (January 2026):
- Key figure in transition discussions
- International support for her leadership
- Uncertain path to formal power

## Nobel Prize

### Award
- Nobel Peace Prize for pro-democracy work
- International recognition of her resistance to authoritarianism

### The Medal Incident
In January 2026, Machado gave her **physical Nobel medal** to Trump:
- Gesture of gratitude for Operation Absolute Resolve
- Trump appeared to conflate having the medal with being a laureate
- Nobel Committee clarified: laureate status cannot be transferred
- The medal itself can be gifted or sold, but not the honor
- Historical precedent: some laureates have sold medals (e.g., for charity)

## Significance

### For Venezuela
- Symbol of democratic resistance
- Potential future leader
- Represents continuity of opposition movement

### For US Policy
- Trump administration's preferred outcome
- Validates intervention narrative
- Personifies "liberation" framing
