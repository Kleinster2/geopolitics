# Lowy Institute

## Overview
Australia's leading foreign policy think tank, focused on Australia's role in Asia-Pacific and the world. Known for Asia Power Index (ranking regional power), quality analysis, and independent voice. Punches above weight internationally. Critical lens on Australian policy while engaging constructively.

## Key Facts
- **Founded**: 2003
- **Founder**: Frank Lowy (Westfield billionaire)
- **Headquarters**: Sydney
- **Executive Director**: Michael Fullilove
- **Focus**: Australian foreign policy, Asia-Pacific
