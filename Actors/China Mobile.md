# China Mobile

China's largest state-owned telecom operator (~1.0bn mobile customers). Geopolitically relevant as the first of the three Chinese telecom ADRs delisted from the NYSE under [[Executive Order 13959]] (CHL, January 2021) — a marquee case of US capital-markets decoupling. Controlled by SASAC; co-operator of the [[Chile-China Express]] submarine cable.
