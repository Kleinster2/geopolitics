# Malaysia

## Overview
Multi-ethnic Southeast Asian nation straddling strategic Strait of Malacca. Muslim-majority but officially multi-religious. Former British colony with Westminster-style system under unique rotating monarchy. Balances between US and China while managing domestic ethnic politics. Key ASEAN member with South China Sea claims.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Kuala Lumpur (administrative: Putrajaya)
- **Population**: ~34 million
- **GDP**: ~$430 billion
- **PM**: Anwar Ibrahim (2022-)
- **Monarchy**: Rotating among 9 sultanates
- **Religion**: Islam (official), multi-religious
