# Emmanuel Macron

## Overview
President of France (2017-present). Advocate of European strategic autonomy and independent defense capability. Key figure in [[European Nuclear Debate]], [[Ukraine Crisis]] response, and European resistance to Trump 2.0 unilateralism.

## Positions

### Foreign Policy (On-the-Record)
- European strategic autonomy - less dependence on US
- French nuclear deterrent as potential European umbrella
- Direct engagement with both Russia and US
- Troop deployment to Ukraine discussions

### Board of Peace Response
Declined invitation. Part of Western pushback bloc alongside UK and Norway.

### European Defense
- Proposed extending French nuclear umbrella to EU allies
- Pushed for European defense spending increases
- [[The Hague Summit 2025]] - Navigated Trump's 5% GDP demand

### Ukraine
- Among leaders discussing European troop deployment ([[Ukraine Crisis]])
- Early advocate of not ruling out ground forces
