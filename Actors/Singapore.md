# Singapore

## Overview
Wealthy city-state at the tip of Malay Peninsula controlling the Strait of Malacca. Punches far above its weight through strategic positioning, financial hub status, and pragmatic diplomacy. Balances US security ties with China economic engagement. Model of successful development, though with constrained political freedoms.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Singapore (city-state)
- **Population**: ~5.9 million
- **GDP**: ~$500 billion
- **GDP per capita**: ~$85,000 (among world's highest)
- **PM**: Lawrence Wong (2024-)
- **Ruling Party**: People's Action Party (since 1959)
