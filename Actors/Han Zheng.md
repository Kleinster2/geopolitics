## June 2026 UK meeting

On June 2, 2026, Han met UK Foreign Secretary [[Yvette Cooper]] in Beijing during the same visit that included Cooper's 11th China-UK Strategic Dialogue with [[Wang Yi]]. China Daily reported that Han framed the meeting around true multilateralism, global governance reform, and stability in a turbulent international environment.
