# Bank Mellat

## Overview
Major Iranian state-owned bank, one of the largest in the country. Subject to international sanctions but has had some legal victories in challenging EU designations.

## Key Facts
- **Persian name**: بانک ملت
- **Meaning**: "Bank of the Nation"
- **Established**: 1980 (merger of 10 pre-revolution banks)
- **Type**: State-owned commercial bank
- **Status**: Operating, sanctioned

## Legal Challenges
Bank Mellat notably challenged EU sanctions in European courts and won some cases, arguing designations were procedurally flawed. However, remains under US sanctions.

## Current Status (2026)
Part of the fragile [[Iranian Banking System]], affected by:
- Rial collapse
- Economic crisis
- Sanctions pressure
- Systemic banking problems
