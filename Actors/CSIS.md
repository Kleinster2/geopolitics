# Center for Strategic and International Studies (CSIS)

## Overview
Major Washington think tank focused on defense, security, and international affairs. Known for bipartisan approach and convening power. Strong Asia and defense programs. Combines research with high-profile events and media presence. Prominent voice on great power competition.

## Key Facts
- **Founded**: 1962
- **Headquarters**: Washington, DC
- **Staff**: ~200 scholars
- **Budget**: ~$50 million
- **President/CEO**: John Hamre (former Deputy SecDef)

## History

### Origins
- Founded at Georgetown University
- Cold War security focus
- Became independent (1987)
- Bipartisan from start

### Evolution

- Asia expansion

- Media/convening emphasis
