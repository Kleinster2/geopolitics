# Royal United Services Institute (RUSI)

## Overview
World's oldest defense and security think tank, founded under Duke of Wellington's patronage. British defense establishment institution. Known for operational military analysis, particularly prominent during Ukraine war. Combines historical prestige with relevant contemporary analysis.

## Key Facts
- **Founded**: 1831
- **Headquarters**: Whitehall, London
- **Director-General**: Karin von Hippel (2024-)
- **Patron**: King Charles III
- **Focus**: Defense, security, military affairs
