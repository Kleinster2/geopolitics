# Middle East Institute (MEI)

## Overview
Washington's oldest Middle East-focused think tank. Broad regional coverage from Morocco to Pakistan. Known for balanced analysis, language training, and cultural programming. Less advocacy-oriented than some regional peers. Provides platform for diverse perspectives including Arab voices often underrepresented in DC.

## Key Facts
- **Founded**: 1946
- **Headquarters**: Washington, DC
- **President**: Paul Salem
- **Focus**: Middle East and North Africa
- **Distinction**: Oldest US Middle East think tank
