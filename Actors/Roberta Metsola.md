# Roberta Metsola

## Overview
President of the European Parliament since January 2022 (EPP, Malta). Re-elected July 2024 for second term. Youngest EP President in history at election. Presides over Parliament during contentious debates on trade deals, Ukraine support, and EU institutional reform.

## Key Facts
- **Position**: President of the European Parliament
- **Party**: Partit Nazzjonalista (EPP group)
- **Country**: Malta
- **In Office**: January 2022-present (second term from July 2024)
- **Born**: 1979

## Role

### Parliamentary Leadership
- Presides over EP plenary sessions
- Represents Parliament in inter-institutional negotiations
- Signs EU legislation into law alongside Council President

### Key Votes Overseen
- [[EU-Mercosur FTA]] referral to Court of Justice (334-324, November 2025)
- Ukraine support packages
- EU budget negotiations

## Political Position
- Center-right (EPP)
- Pro-European integration
- Strong supporter of rule of law
- Previously criticized for abortion stance (evolved position)

## Significance
Represents small member state in top EU role. Her presidency spans critical period of EU institutional stress, trade controversies, and response to geopolitical crises.
