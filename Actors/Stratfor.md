# Stratfor

## Overview
Private intelligence and geopolitical analysis firm founded by George Friedman. Known for applying geopolitical frameworks to current events. Gained notoriety from WikiLeaks hack (2012). Now operates as RANE (Risk Assistance Network + Exchange) after 2015 acquisition. Influenced how geopolitics is communicated to general audiences.

## Key Facts
- **Founded**: 1996
- **Founder**: George Friedman
- **Location**: Austin, Texas
- **Current**: Part of RANE (since 2015)
- **Known for**: Daily intelligence briefs
