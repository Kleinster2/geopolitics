# Abelardo de la Espriella

Colombian-Italian lawyer and far-right politician. Founder of *Defensores de la Patria* (Defenders of the Homeland). 2026 presidential candidate.

## Background

- **Born**: July 31, 1978
- **Education**: Law specialization (Universidad del Rosario), Master's in Law (Nebrija University)
- **Profession**: Criminal defense lawyer

### AUC Connection

Former advisor to the [[AUC|United Self-Defense Forces of Colombia (AUC)]], a far-right paramilitary organization and drug cartel designated as terrorist by the US and EU. This remains a significant point of controversy.

## 2026 Presidential Campaign

### Candidacy Launch

Launched candidacy at Movistar Arena in Bogotá before 15,000+ supporters at the national convention of Defensores de la Patria.

### Signature Collection

Certified by the Registraduría Nacional (National Civil Registry) on January 20, 2026. Submitted 4,869,407 signatures—the highest of any candidate qualifying via signatures.

### Political Support

According to Blu Radio (January 2026), de la Espriella has attracted support from:
- **Creemos** - Movement founded by Federico Gutiérrez ("Fico")
- **Cambio Radical** faction - Char family
- **Salvación Nacional** - Led by Enrique Gómez
- Senator Mauricio Gómez Amín (Liberal)
- Former Justice Minister Wilson Ruiz

### Policy Positions

**Drug Policy**: In November 2025, proposed controversial amnesty scheme for Colombian drug traffickers.

## Electoral Timeline

- **First round**: May 31, 2026

## Significance

De la Espriella's rise represents a potential sharp rightward turn after Petro's historic left-wing presidency. His paramilitary ties and drug amnesty proposal make him a polarizing figure domestically and complicate US-Colombia relations regardless of outcome.
