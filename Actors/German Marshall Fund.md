# German Marshall Fund (GMF)

## Overview
Transatlantic think tank dedicated to strengthening US-European cooperation. Created as German gift commemorating Marshall Plan. Offices on both sides of Atlantic. Focus on democracy, security, and transatlantic ties. Increasingly engaged on China and Indo-Pacific as transatlantic agenda expands.

## Key Facts
- **Founded**: 1972
- **Origin**: German gift for Marshall Plan anniversary
- **Headquarters**: Washington, DC
- **Offices**: Berlin, Brussels, Ankara, Belgrade, Paris, Warsaw, Bucharest
- **President**: Heather Conley (2021-)
