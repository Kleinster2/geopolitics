# Hassan Rashad

## Overview
Egyptian Major General. Member of the [[Board of Peace]] Gaza Executive Board (GEB). Egypt is a key actor in Gaza affairs given its shared border (Rafah crossing) and role in ceasefire mediation.

## Positions

### Current
- Member, Board of Peace Gaza Executive Board

## Board of Peace Role
Military representative reflecting Egypt's security interests in Gaza. Egypt controls the Rafah border crossing, has been central to ceasefire negotiations, and has security concerns about instability on its Sinai border.
