# Hamas

## Overview
Palestinian Sunni Islamist movement controlling Gaza since 2007. Combines political governance, social services, and military wing (Qassam Brigades). October 7, 2023 attack on Israel triggered devastating war. Part of [[Axis of Resistance]] despite Sunni-Shia divide.

## Basic Facts
- **Full Name**: Harakat al-Muqawama al-Islamiyya (Islamic Resistance Movement)
- **Founded**: 1987 (First Intifada)
- **Ideology**: Sunni Islamist, Palestinian nationalist
- **Leader**: Yahya Sinwar (killed 2024), Ismail Haniyeh (killed 2024)
- **Military Wing**: Izz ad-Din al-Qassam Brigades
- **Gaza Population**: ~2 million

## Origins

### Muslim Brotherhood Roots
- Gaza branch of Muslim Brotherhood
- Sheikh Ahmed Yassin founder
- Initially tolerated by Israel (counter to PLO)
- 1987: Formalized as Hamas

### First Intifada (1987-1993)
- Mass uprising participation
- Gained credibility
- Suicide bombing campaign began

### Post-Oslo
- Opposed peace process
- Rejected two-state solution
- Violent resistance continued

## Gaza Takeover (2006-2007)

### 2006 Elections
- Won Palestinian legislative elections
- International isolation followed
- Sanctions imposed

### 2007 Conflict
- Hamas-Fatah civil war
- Hamas seized Gaza control
- West Bank/Gaza split
- Blockade imposed

## Governance

### Gaza Control
- De facto government
- Security forces
- Civil administration
- Tax collection

### Challenges
- Israeli/Egyptian blockade
- Economic collapse
- Infrastructure destruction
- International isolation

## Military Capabilities

### Qassam Brigades
- 25,000-40,000 fighters

## Iran Relationship

### Complicated Alliance
- Sunni movement + Shia Iran
- Syrian civil war tensions (Hamas backed rebels)
- Reconciliation 2017+
- October 7 coordination debated

### Support
- $100M/year (historical estimates)
- Weapons technology
- Training
- Political support

## October 7, 2023 Attack

### Operation "Al-Aqsa Flood"
- Surprise mass attack
- ~1,200 Israelis killed
- ~250 hostages taken
- Unprecedented breach
- Multiple failure points (Israeli intelligence)

### Planning
- Years of preparation
- Secrecy maintained
- Iranian involvement debated
- Sophisticated operation

### Aftermath
- Israeli invasion of Gaza
- Massive destruction
- Tens of thousands dead
- Hamas degraded but surviving
- Regional escalation
