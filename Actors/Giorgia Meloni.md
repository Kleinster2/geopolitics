# Giorgia Meloni

## Overview
Prime Minister of Italy (2022-present). Leader of Fratelli d'Italia (Brothers of Italy). First female Italian PM. Positioned herself as bridge between Trump's US and European establishment.

## Positions

### Foreign Policy (On-the-Record)
- Pro-NATO, pro-Ukraine (distinguishing from far-right peers)
- Strong US-Italy relationship, personal rapport with Trump
- Mediterranean and Africa migration focus
- Energy diversification from Russia

### Board of Peace Response
"Ready to do our part" - among enthusiastic early supporters. Consistent with her approach of engaging closely with Trump while maintaining European credibility.

### Trump Relationship
- Among first European leaders to build rapport with Trump 2.0
- Positioned as potential US-Europe intermediary
- Contrast with Macron/Starmer distancing
