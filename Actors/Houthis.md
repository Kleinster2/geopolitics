# Houthis (Ansar Allah)

## Overview
Yemeni rebel movement controlling much of northern Yemen, including capital Sanaa. Initially a local Zaydi Shia revivalist group, now Iran-aligned member of [[Axis of Resistance]]. Gained global attention through Red Sea shipping attacks following October 7, 2023.

## Basic Facts
- **Official Name**: Ansar Allah (Supporters of God)
- **Common Name**: Houthis (after founding family)
- **Founded**: 1990s (Believing Youth movement)
- **Leader**: Abdul-Malik al-Houthi
- **Territory**: ~70% of Yemen's population
- **Slogan**: "God is Great, Death to America, Death to Israel, Curse on the Jews, Victory to Islam"

## Origins

### Zaydi Revival (1990s)
- Zaydi Shia minority (~35% of Yemen)
- Hussein al-Houthi founded movement
- Cultural/religious revival initially
- Government marginalization

### Saada Wars (2004-2010)
- Six rounds of conflict
- Hussein al-Houthi killed (2004)
- Brother Abdul-Malik takes over
- Grievance deepening

### Arab Spring & Rise (2011-2014)
- Exploited transition chaos
- Alliance with Saleh (former enemy)
- September 2014: Seized Sanaa
- Triggered Saudi intervention (2015)

## Yemen Civil War

### Conflict Lines
- Houthis/Saleh vs. Hadi government
- Saudi-led coalition intervention (2015)
- UAE involvement (south)
- Saleh killed (2017) - switched sides

### Current Situation
- De facto north-south division
- Saudi seeking exit
- UN mediation ongoing
- No resolution

### Humanitarian Crisis
- 150,000+ deaths
- Millions displaced
- Famine conditions
- "World's worst humanitarian crisis"
