# Brookings Institution

## Overview
America's oldest and one of its most influential think tanks. Known for rigorous, academic-style research across domestic and foreign policy. Generally center-left orientation but maintains bipartisan credibility. Major pipeline for Democratic administrations. Headquartered on Massachusetts Avenue's "Think Tank Row."

## Key Facts
- **Founded**: 1916
- **Headquarters**: Washington, DC
- **Staff**: ~300 scholars
- **Budget**: ~$100 million annually
- **President**: Cecilia Rouse (2023-)

## History

### Origins
- Robert Brookings (businessman/philanthropist)
- Government efficiency focus initially
- Merged three institutions (1927)
- Progressive Era roots
