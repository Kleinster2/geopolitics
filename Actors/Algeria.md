# Algeria

## Overview
Largest African country by area with significant gas reserves increasingly important to Europe post-Russia. Military-dominated authoritarian system resistant to Arab Spring. Historic non-aligned leader. Rivalry with Morocco over Western Sahara. Strategic Mediterranean position. Cautious foreign policy prioritizing sovereignty and non-interference.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Algiers
- **Population**: ~45 million
- **GDP**: ~$195 billion
- **President**: Abdelmadjid Tebboune (2019-)
- **Independence**: 1962 (from France)
- **Key Export**: Natural gas
