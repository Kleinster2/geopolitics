# Thailand

## Overview
US treaty ally and ASEAN founder caught between military establishment and democratic aspirations. Only Southeast Asian nation never colonized. Revered monarchy central to national identity. Strategic location between South and Southeast Asia. Chronic political instability with recurring coups, now navigating new political era.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Bangkok
- **Population**: ~72 million
- **GDP**: ~$535 billion
- **King**: Vajiralongkorn (Rama X, 2016-)
- **PM**: Paetongtarn Shinawatra (2024-)
- **US Treaty**: 1954 Manila Pact
