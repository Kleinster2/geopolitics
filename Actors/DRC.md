# Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC)

## Overview
Vast Central African nation with immense mineral wealth but chronic instability. Critical source of cobalt, copper, and coltan essential for green energy transition and electronics. Decades of conflict in eastern regions with multiple armed groups. Weak state capacity despite resource wealth. Geopolitical significance growing as critical minerals demand surges.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Kinshasa
- **Population**: ~102 million (4th in Africa)
- **GDP**: ~$65 billion
- **President**: Félix Tshisekedi (2019-)
- **Size**: 2.3 million km² (size of Western Europe)
- **Former Colonial Power**: Belgium
