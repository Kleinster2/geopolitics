# Indonesia

## Overview
World's largest archipelagic state, largest Muslim-majority country, and ASEAN's natural leader. Strategic location controlling key sea lanes. Rising economic power maintaining non-aligned foreign policy tradition. Critical swing state in US-China competition.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Jakarta (moving to Nusantara, Borneo)
- **Population**: ~277 million (4th largest)
- **GDP**: ~$1.4 trillion
- **Islands**: 17,000+ (6,000 inhabited)
- **Religion**: 87% Muslim
- **President**: Prabowo Subianto (2024-)

## Geopolitical Position

### Strategic Geography

- Maritime chokepoints
