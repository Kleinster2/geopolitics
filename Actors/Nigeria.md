# Nigeria

## Overview
Africa's most populous country and largest economy, wielding significant regional influence through ECOWAS. Oil-dependent economy facing security threats from Boko Haram, banditry, and separatism. Demographic giant with continental leadership aspirations.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Abuja
- **Population**: ~220 million (Africa's largest; 7th globally)
- **GDP**: ~$475 billion (Africa's largest)
- **Oil Production**: ~1.5 million bpd
- **President**: Head of state and government
- **Structure**: 36 states + FCT

## Security Challenges

### Boko Haram / ISWAP
- Northeast insurgency (since 2009)
- Thousands killed, millions displaced
- Islamic State affiliation (split)
- Military operations ongoing
- Humanitarian crisis

### Banditry (Northwest)
- Kidnapping epidemic
- Herder-farmer conflicts
- Criminal networks
- Mass abductions (schools)
- Limited state control

### Separatism
- IPOB (Biafra secessionism)
- Southeast unrest
- Historical civil war trauma
- Sit-at-home orders
- Nnamdi Kanu detention

### Oil Theft / Niger Delta
- Massive crude theft
- Militant legacy
- Environmental devastation
- Pipeline sabotage
- Revenue losses
