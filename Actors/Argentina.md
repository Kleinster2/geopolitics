# Argentina

## Overview
South America's second-largest economy plagued by chronic economic crises and political volatility. Significant lithium reserves positioning it in critical minerals competition. Javier Milei's radical libertarian presidency marks sharp ideological shift. G20 member with historical regional influence now diminished. Falklands/Malvinas dispute with UK shapes national identity.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Buenos Aires
- **Population**: ~46 million
- **GDP**: ~$640 billion (high inflation distorts)
- **President**: Javier Milei (2023-)
- **Key Resources**: Lithium, agriculture, shale gas
