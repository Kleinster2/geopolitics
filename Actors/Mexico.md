# Mexico

## Overview
Latin America's second-largest economy with unique position as US southern neighbor. Deeply integrated with North American economy through USMCA. Struggles with cartel violence while serving as migration corridor. Major manufacturing hub and emerging power.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Mexico City
- **Population**: ~130 million
- **GDP**: ~$1.4 trillion
- **President**: (six-year term, no reelection)
- **Border**: 2,000 miles with United States
