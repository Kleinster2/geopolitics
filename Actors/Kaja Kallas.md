# Kaja Kallas

## Overview
EU High Representative for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy since December 2024. Former Estonian Prime Minister (2021-2024). Known for hawkish stance on Russia and strong support for Ukraine. First Baltic leader to hold EU's top foreign policy position.

## Key Facts
- **Position**: High Representative of the EU for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy
- **Previous**: Prime Minister of Estonia (2021-2024)
- **Party**: Estonian Reform Party (liberal)
- **In Office**: December 2024-present

## Background
- Daughter of Siim Kallas (former EU Commissioner, Estonian PM)
- Lawyer by training
- Led Estonia during Russian invasion of Ukraine
- Sanctioned by Russia (travel ban)

## Policy Positions

### Russia & Ukraine
- Strongly hawkish on Russia
- Advocates maximum support for Ukraine
- Personal experience: family deported to Siberia under Soviet rule
- Pushed for strongest possible EU sanctions

### European Defense
- Supports European strategic autonomy
- Advocates increased defense spending
- Coordinates EU foreign policy during US retrenchment

### Transatlantic Relations
- Navigating EU-US tensions under Trump 2.0
- Balancing European interests with NATO alliance

## Significance
First Baltic state representative to hold EU's top foreign policy role. Appointment signals EU's eastward tilt and prioritization of Russia threat following 2022 invasion.
