# Washington Institute for Near East Policy (WINEP)

## Overview
Influential Washington think tank focused on US policy toward Middle East. Known for Israel expertise and generally pro-Israel orientation. AIPAC origins. Major voice on Iran policy, Arab-Israeli issues, and Gulf affairs. Significant influence on US Middle East policy across administrations.

## Key Facts
- **Founded**: 1985
- **Headquarters**: Washington, DC
- **Executive Director**: Robert Satloff
- **Origin**: AIPAC-linked founding
- **Focus**: US Middle East policy
