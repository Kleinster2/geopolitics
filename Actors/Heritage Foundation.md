# Heritage Foundation

## Overview
America's most influential conservative think tank. Known for advocacy-oriented research and deep ties to Republican administrations. Pioneered the rapid-response policy brief model. Published the "Mandate for Leadership" blueprint used by Reagan and Trump administrations. More ideological and activist than traditional academic think tanks.

## Key Facts
- **Founded**: 1973
- **Headquarters**: Washington, DC
- **President**: Kevin Roberts (2021-)
- **Budget**: ~$80+ million
- **Orientation**: Conservative
