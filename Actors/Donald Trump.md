# Donald Trump

## Overview
45th and 47th President of the United States. Second term began January 2025. Central actor in multiple vault flashpoints including the [[Greenland Crisis]], [[Board of Peace]], [[Trade War 2025-2026]], and [[Venezuela]] intervention.

## Positions (Trump 2.0)

### Foreign Policy Approach
- Bilateral deal-making over multilateral institutions
- Transactional relationships with allies and adversaries
- Willingness to threaten NATO allies (Greenland, trade)
- Personal diplomacy with strongmen (Putin, Xi, Orban)

### Key Actions (2025-2026)
- [[Alaska Summit 2025]] - Summit with Putin (August 2025)
- [[The Hague Summit 2025]] - Pushed NATO to 5% GDP target
- [[Trade War 2025-2026]] - "Liberation Day" tariffs
- [[Greenland Crisis]] - Annexation threats against Denmark
- [[Board of Peace]] - Launched at Davos (January 2026)
- [[2025 National Security Strategy]] - Four-theater framework
- Venezuela intervention (Operation Absolute Resolve)

### Board of Peace Role
- Holds ultimate decision-making power per charter
- Initially proposed as Gaza reconstruction body
- Expanded to broader conflict-resolution mandate
- Suggested it "might replace" the [[United Nations]], later backtracked
