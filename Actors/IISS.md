# International Institute for Strategic Studies (IISS)

## Overview
London-based think tank focused on defense, security, and strategic affairs. Known for authoritative data publications including *The Military Balance* (global defense reference) and *Strategic Survey*. Hosts prestigious Shangri-La Dialogue in Singapore. More data-driven and defense-technical than policy-advocacy oriented.

## Key Facts
- **Founded**: 1958
- **Headquarters**: London
- **Director-General**: Bastian Giegerich (2022-)
- **Offices**: London, Singapore, Washington, Bahrain, Berlin
- **Known For**: *Military Balance*, Shangri-La Dialogue
