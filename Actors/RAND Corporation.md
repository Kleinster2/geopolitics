# RAND Corporation

## Overview
Pioneering defense research institution that invented the modern think tank model. Born from Air Force need for strategic analysis during early Cold War. Known for rigorous quantitative methodology, game theory applications, and classified government research. "RAND" = Research ANd Development.

## Key Facts
- **Founded**: 1948
- **Origin**: Spun off from Douglas Aircraft
- **Headquarters**: Santa Monica, CA
- **Staff**: ~1,900 researchers
- **Budget**: ~$350 million
- **Clients**: Primarily US government

## Origins

### Post-WWII Context
- "Project RAND" at Douglas Aircraft (1945)
- Air Force strategic analysis needs
- Nuclear strategy questions
- Became independent (1948)

### Cold War Role
- Nuclear deterrence theory
- Systems analysis
- Game theory applications
- Defense planning
