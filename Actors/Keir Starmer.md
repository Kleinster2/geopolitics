# Keir Starmer

## Overview
Prime Minister of the United Kingdom (2024-present). Former Leader of the Opposition (2020-2024). Former Director of Public Prosecutions (2008-2013). Labour Party leader who won 2024 general election.

## Positions

### Foreign Policy (On-the-Record)
- Committed to NATO and transatlantic alliance
- Supported Ukraine with troop deployment discussions
- Navigating Trump 2.0 relationship while maintaining European alignment

### Board of Peace Response
Declined to endorse. Cited Vladimir Putin's involvement as "concerning." Positioned UK with France and Norway in declining.

### Ukraine
- UK among nations discussing troop deployment ([[Ukraine Crisis]])
- Continued predecessor's strong Ukraine support
