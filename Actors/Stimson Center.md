# Stimson Center

## Overview
Washington think tank focused on peace, security, and arms control. Named for Henry Stimson (FDR's Secretary of War). Known for pragmatic security research, South Asia expertise, and environmental security work. Less hawkish than some peers, with focus on conflict prevention and risk reduction.

## Key Facts
- **Founded**: 1989
- **Named For**: Henry L. Stimson
- **Headquarters**: Washington, DC
- **President**: Brian Finlay
- **Focus**: Security, arms control, peace
