# Bruegel

## Overview
Leading European economic policy think tank based in Brussels. Focus on EU economic governance, monetary policy, digital economy, and energy/climate. Known for rigorous, data-driven analysis. Influential in European Commission and ECB circles. Bridge between academic economics and EU policy-making.

## Key Facts
- **Founded**: 2005
- **Headquarters**: Brussels
- **Director**: Jeromin Zettelmeyer (2022-)
- **Focus**: European and global economics
- **Name**: From "Brussels European and Global Economic Laboratory"
