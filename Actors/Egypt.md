# Egypt

## Overview
Most populous Arab state and historic regional leader now facing economic crisis and diminished influence. Controls Suez Canal, critical global chokepoint. Major US aid recipient maintaining cold peace with Israel. Under Sisi, returned to military-backed authoritarianism after brief Arab Spring democratic experiment.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Cairo
- **Population**: ~105 million (largest Arab state)
- **GDP**: ~$475 billion
- **President**: Abdel Fattah el-Sisi (2014-)
- **System**: Military-dominated republic
- **Strategic Asset**: Suez Canal
