# South Africa

## Overview
Africa's most industrialized economy and only African G20 member. Post-apartheid democracy facing severe domestic challenges while maintaining continental leadership aspirations. BRICS member balancing Western and non-Western ties.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Pretoria (executive), Cape Town (legislative), Bloemfontein (judicial)
- **Population**: ~60 million
- **GDP**: ~$400 billion (Africa's 2nd largest)
- **Ruling Party**: ANC (since 1994)
- **President**: Head of state and government

## Historical Context

### Apartheid Era (1948-1994)
- Racial segregation system
- International isolation
- Sanctions regime
- Armed resistance (ANC)

### Democratic Transition (1994)
- Mandela's reconciliation
- ANC dominance
- Constitutional democracy
- International reintegration
