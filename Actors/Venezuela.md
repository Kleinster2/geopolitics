# Venezuela

## Overview
Once wealthy oil state now in prolonged humanitarian and political crisis under Maduro regime. Largest refugee crisis in Western Hemisphere. Contested 2024 election revealed continued authoritarian grip despite opposition victory claims. US sanctions and international pressure have failed to dislodge regime. China and Russia provide lifelines.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Caracas
- **Population**: ~28 million (down from 30M+)
- **GDP**: ~$100 billion (collapsed from $350B+)
- **President**: Nicolás Maduro (2013-, disputed)
- **Opposition Leader**: María Corina Machado
- **Oil Reserves**: Largest in world (proven)
