# Hezbollah (Party of God)

## Overview
Iran's most powerful and capable proxy, functioning as both Lebanon's dominant political force and a military organization stronger than the Lebanese state. Created by [[IRGC]] in 1982, Hezbollah maintains 150,000+ rockets aimed at Israel and serves as Iran's primary deterrent.

## Basic Facts
- **Arabic**: حزب الله (Ḥizb Allāh)
- **Founded**: 1982 (Israeli invasion of Lebanon)
- **Leader**: Hassan Nasrallah (1992-2024, killed); successor TBD
- **Political Role**: Major Lebanese political party
- **Military**: Estimated 30,000-50,000 fighters
- **Rockets/Missiles**: 150,000+ (largest non-state arsenal)
