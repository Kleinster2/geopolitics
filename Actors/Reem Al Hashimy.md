# Reem Al Hashimy

## Overview
UAE State Minister for International Cooperation. Member of the [[Board of Peace]] Gaza Executive Board (GEB). Previously served as Director-General of Expo 2020 Dubai.

## Positions

### Current
- **State Minister for International Cooperation, UAE**
- Member, Board of Peace Gaza Executive Board

## Board of Peace Role
Represents UAE's interests in Gaza reconstruction and development. The UAE is an Abraham Accords signatory (normalized relations with Israel in 2020) and has positioned itself as a reconstruction and development partner for post-conflict Gaza.
