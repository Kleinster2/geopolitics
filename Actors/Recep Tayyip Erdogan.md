# Recep Tayyip Erdogan

## Overview
President of Turkey (2014-present; PM 2003-2014). Longest-serving Turkish leader in modern era. Positions Turkey as bridge between NATO and Russia, West and Islamic world. [[Board of Peace]] signatory.

## Positions

### Foreign Policy (On-the-Record)
- Turkey as independent regional power, not subordinate NATO ally
- Maintained relations with both Russia and Ukraine during war
- Purchased Russian S-400 system despite US objections (2019)
- Grain deal mediation between Russia and Ukraine (2022)
- Military operations in Syria, Iraq, Libya
- Support for Hamas/Palestinian cause, criticism of Israel

### NATO Dynamics
- Blocked Sweden's NATO accession (delayed until 2024)
- S-400 purchase triggered US F-35 expulsion
- Strategic location gives Turkey leverage within alliance
- Balances NATO membership with Russian relationship

### Board of Peace
Turkey signed as member. Consistent with Erdogan's pattern of engaging all sides and positioning Turkey as indispensable mediator.

### Israel-Palestine
- Strong rhetorical support for Palestinians
- Recalled ambassador from Israel over Gaza war
- Netanyahu noted Turkish officials on Gaza committee as concern
