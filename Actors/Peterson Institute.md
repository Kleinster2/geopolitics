# Peterson Institute for International Economics

## Overview
Premier think tank for international economic policy. Free trade advocacy, globalization analysis, and macroeconomic research. Known for rigorous quantitative work on trade, currency, and development. Strong voice for open markets, now adapting to industrial policy era. Highly influential in economic policy circles.

## Key Facts
- **Founded**: 1981 (as Institute for International Economics)
- **Renamed**: 2006 (for Peter G. Peterson)
- **Headquarters**: Washington, DC
- **President**: Adam Posen
- **Focus**: International economics
