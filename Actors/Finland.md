# Finland

## Overview
Nordic nation that ended decades of neutrality by joining NATO in 2023 following Russia's invasion of Ukraine. Shares 1,340 km border with Russia - now NATO's longest direct frontier with Moscow. Militarily capable with strong territorial defense tradition. Deploying forces to Greenland in 2026 crisis.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Helsinki
- **Population**: 5.5 million
- **GDP**: ~$300 billion
- **Government**: Parliamentary republic
- **President**: Alexander Stubb (2024-)
- **PM**: Petteri Orpo (2023-)
- **NATO Member**: April 2023

## Strategic Position

### Russia Border
- **1,340 km** shared border - NATO's longest with Russia
- Historical "Finlandization" during Cold War
- 2022 invasion ended neutrality consensus
- Now front-line NATO state

### Arctic
- Northern territory above Arctic Circle
- Arctic Council member
- Resource and shipping interests
- Climate change impacts

## Military

### Capabilities
- **Wartime Strength**: 280,000 (one of Europe's largest)
- **Reserve**: 900,000 trained reservists
- **Conscription**: Universal male service
- **Defense Spending**: ~2% GDP (rising)

### Key Assets
- F-35 Lightning II (on order, replacing F/A-18 Hornets)
- Strong artillery capability
- Extensive fortifications
- Winter warfare expertise
- Cyber defense

### Defense Doctrine
- Total defense concept
- Territorial integrity focus
- Rapid mobilization capability
- Integrated with NATO since 2023

## NATO Membership

### Historic Shift
- 75+ years of neutrality ended
- Public opinion shifted after Feb 2022
- Applied May 2022
- Joined April 4, 2023
- Sweden followed in 2024

### Implications
- Doubles NATO-Russia border
- Baltic Sea now "NATO lake"
- Arctic dimension added
- Russian strategic concern

## 2026 Greenland Deployment

### Military Liaison Officers
- Finland sending officers to Greenland
- Supporting Denmark against US annexation threats
- Part of European solidarity response
- Unprecedented: Supporting NATO ally against NATO ally

### Significance
- Shows European unity
- Arctic expertise valuable
- Tests alliance dynamics
