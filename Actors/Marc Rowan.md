# Marc Rowan

## Overview
American billionaire investor. Co-founder and CEO of Apollo Global Management. Member of the [[Board of Peace]] founding executive board and the National Committee for the Administration of Gaza. Wharton graduate (BA 1984, MBA 1985). Major Republican donor.

## Positions

### Board of Peace
- Member, founding executive board
- Member, National Committee for the Administration of Gaza (day-to-day operations in the territory)
- Dual role on both the strategic board and the operational Gaza administration

### Apollo Global Management
- **Co-founder and CEO** - One of the world's largest alternative asset managers
- Net worth in the billions

### Trump Relationship
- Reportedly considered for Secretary of the Treasury (2024)
- Active Republican and Trump supporter
- Worked with White House on preferential funding compact sent to universities (October 2025)

## Relevance
Rowan's inclusion represents the private finance dimension of the Board of Peace. His role on both the executive board and the Gaza administration committee positions him at the intersection of strategic vision and operational implementation. His pro-Israel background and financial expertise suggest a focus on investment-led reconstruction.
