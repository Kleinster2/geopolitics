# Chile

## Overview
South America's most stable and developed economy, though recent social unrest revealed deep inequalities. World's largest copper producer and major lithium source - critical for energy transition. Long Pacific coastline with Antarctic claims. Traditionally US-aligned with market-oriented policies, now navigating leftist Boric government.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Santiago
- **Population**: ~19 million
- **GDP**: ~$335 billion
- **President**: Gabriel Boric (2022-)
- **GDP per capita**: Highest in South America
- **Key Export**: Copper (50%+ of exports)
