# Council on Foreign Relations

## Overview
America's most prestigious foreign policy think tank and membership organization. Publishes *Foreign Affairs*, the most influential international relations journal. Represents the US foreign policy establishmentâ€”criticized as both essential institution and embodiment of elite groupthink. Where American grand strategy is debated and shaped.

- **Founded**: 1921
