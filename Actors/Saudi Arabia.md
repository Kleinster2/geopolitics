# Saudi Arabia

## Overview
World's largest oil exporter and custodian of Islam's holiest sites. Under Crown Prince MBS, pursuing ambitious modernization (Vision 2030) while navigating regional rivalries and evolving US relationship.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Riyadh
- **Population**: ~35 million
- **GDP**: ~$1.1 trillion
- **Oil Production**: ~10 million barrels/day
- **King**: Salman bin Abdulaziz
- **De facto ruler**: Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman (MBS)

## Geopolitical Position

### Regional Leadership
- Leading Sunni Arab power
- Custodian of Two Holy Mosques (Mecca, Medina)
- Arab League influence
- Counter-Iran coalition builder

### Vision 2030
Massive economic/social transformation:
- Diversification from oil
- NEOM megaproject
- Entertainment and tourism
- Social liberalization
- Tech investments

## Strategic Priorities
1. Regime security
2. Counter Iranian influence
3. Economic diversification
4. Regional stability
5. Great power hedging
6. Nuclear capability (hedging)

## Key Relationships

### Traditional Allies
- [[United States]] - Security umbrella (strained)
- GCC states - Economic integration
- [[Egypt]] - Arab ally
- [[Pakistan]] - Security ties

### Evolving Relationships
- [[China]] - Major oil customer, investments
- [[Russia]] - OPEC+ coordination
- [[Israel]] - Normalization discussions
- [[Iran]] - Rapprochement attempts (China-brokered)

### Rivals
- [[Iran]] - Regional competition
- Qatar - Recent reconciliation
- Muslim Brotherhood networks

## Military Posture
- Major arms purchaser (US, UK)
- Yemen intervention (costly, inconclusive)
- Limited operational effectiveness
- Nuclear hedging (Pakistan connection, US discussions)

## Economic Factors
- Oil price dependent (despite diversification)
- Sovereign wealth fund (PIF) - $700+ billion
- Massive infrastructure investments
- Youth unemployment challenge

## Internal Dynamics
- MBS consolidation of power
- Social reforms (women driving, entertainment)
- Repression of dissent
- Religious establishment marginalized
- Succession settled (for now)

## Key Challenges
- Yemen exit strategy
- Iran threat management
- Post-oil economy transition
- Human rights criticism
- US reliability concerns
- Demographic pressures
