# Yvette Cooper

Yvette Cooper is the UK Foreign Secretary. In the geopolitics vault, she matters as the Starmer government's senior diplomatic operator on China, India, Ukraine, Iran/Hormuz, Hong Kong, and wider global-crisis coordination.

## June 2026 China visit

Cooper visited China from June 1 to 3, 2026, at [[Wang Yi]]'s invitation and held the 11th China-UK Strategic Dialogue in Beijing on June 2. Reuters reported that the wider China-India trip was aimed at global challenges including the [[Strait of Hormuz]], the Russia-Ukraine war, and Ebola, with Cooper also scheduled for a science-and-technology program in [[Shenzhen]].

The visit is useful evidence for the Starmer government's China formula: cooperate where the UK sees global-stability, trade, climate, AI, and crisis-management value, while still challenging China on security threats, Hong Kong, Taiwan, and infrastructure exposure.
