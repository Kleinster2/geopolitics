# Ali Al-Thawadi

## Overview
Qatari diplomat. Member of the [[Board of Peace]] Gaza Executive Board (GEB). Qatar has played a central role in Gaza ceasefire negotiations and hostage mediation.

## Positions

### Current
- Member, Board of Peace Gaza Executive Board

## Board of Peace Role
Represents Qatar's interests in Gaza reconstruction. Qatar has been a key mediator in Israel-Hamas negotiations and a major donor to Gaza. Netanyahu cited Qatari and Turkish officials on the Gaza committee as a concern for Israeli policy.
