# Tony Blair

## Overview
Former UK Prime Minister (1997-2007). Former Quartet Representative for Middle East Peace (2007-2015). Runs the Tony Blair Institute for Global Change. Member of the [[Board of Peace]] founding executive board.

## Positions

### Board of Peace
- Member, founding executive board (January 2026)
- Originally reported as "appointed executive" in pre-signing coverage

### Previous
- **UK Prime Minister** (1997-2007, Labour)
- **Quartet Representative** for Middle East Peace (2007-2015) - represented UN, US, EU, Russia
- Founded Tony Blair Institute for Global Change (2016)

## Relevance
Blair's appointment connects the Board of Peace to established Middle East peace process experience. His Quartet role gave him experience in the multilateral framework the Board may be attempting to supersede.
