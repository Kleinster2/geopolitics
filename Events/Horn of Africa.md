# Horn of Africa Crisis

## Overview
Strategically critical region facing multiple overlapping crises: Ethiopia's civil war aftermath and regional tensions, Somalia's fragility and al-Shabaab insurgency, Eritrea's isolation and militarization, and competition for Red Sea access. Location at Bab el-Mandeb strait makes it globally significant.

## Strategic Significance

### Geography
- Bab el-Mandeb strait
- Suez Canal southern approach
- Indian Ocean access
- 10% of global trade passes
- See: [[Chokepoints]]

### Global Interest
- Military bases (Djibouti)
- Trade route security
- Migration routes
- Counterterrorism
- Great power competition
