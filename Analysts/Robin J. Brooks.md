# Robin J. Brooks

Senior Fellow at the [[Brookings Institution]]. Former Chief Economist at the Institute of International Finance (IIF). Known for data-driven analysis using real-time indicators (AIS vessel tracking, trade flows, capital movements).

## Relevant analysis

### Strait of Hormuz blockade (March 2026)

Published AIS shipping data showing the de facto blockade's scope during Operation Epic Fury. Key contribution: quantifying the asymmetric cost problem — Iran's navy is destroyed but a few drones can freeze all traffic — and the Russia 2022 comparison (Hormuz ~3x more important for global oil than Russia, yet Brent rise still below the Russia precedent).
