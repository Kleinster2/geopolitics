# Marc Champion

## Overview
Bloomberg Opinion columnist covering Europe, Russia, and the Middle East (based in London). Georgetown University. Nearly 7 years in Moscow including editor-in-chief of The Moscow Times. European News Editor at the Financial Times. 12 years at the Wall Street Journal: London, Brussels (deputy bureau chief), Istanbul (bureau chief). Joined Bloomberg editorial board in 2012.

## Key Positions
- Horn of Africa conflict system is interconnected and solvable with high-level diplomatic engagement
- Trump is uniquely positioned to lead pre-emptive diplomacy in the Horn due to relationships with major players
- Ethiopia's "Two Waters" challenge (Nile + Red Sea) is the core driver that, if resolved as a package, could de-escalate the entire region
