# Elaine McCusker

Former acting Pentagon comptroller / deputy under comptroller in the first Trump administration. Now a defense budget analyst.

## Key assessments

### 2026 Iran conflict — production constraints

Noted that the standoff weapons crucial for high-intensity conflict have persistent manufacturing bottlenecks:

> "High-demand, low-density" precision munitions have a "history of production challenges."

The assessment contextualizes why wartime consumption rates cannot be quickly matched by production ramp-ups — the defense industrial base was not designed for sustained high-intensity operations.

*Source: Bloomberg (March 10-11, 2026)*
