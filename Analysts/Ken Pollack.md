# Ken Pollack

## Overview

Kenneth M. Pollack is a senior analyst at the [[Middle East Institute]], specializing in Middle Eastern military affairs, US foreign policy toward the region, and Iran. Former CIA analyst and National Security Council staffer. Author of multiple books on Middle Eastern security, including *The Threatening Storm* (2002) on Iraq and *Unthinkable* (2013) on Iran policy options.

## Key Facts
- **Affiliation**: [[Middle East Institute]]
