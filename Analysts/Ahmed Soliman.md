# Ahmed Soliman

## Overview
Senior Research Fellow, Africa Programme, [[Chatham House]] (Royal Institute of International Affairs), London. Leads programming on the Horn of Africa. Over a decade of policy-relevant research on Somalia, the Sudans, Ethiopia, and Eritrea. Focuses on political economy and geopolitical connectivity across the Horn and Red Sea region. Also affiliated with the XCEPT research programme. Regular contributor to BBC, Al Jazeera, The Economist, Wall Street Journal, Guardian.

## Key Positions
- Horn of Africa conflicts form a single interconnected "regional conflict system" — alliances shape how conflicts are sustained and metastasize
- The Ethiopia-Eritrea-Tigray crisis cannot be understood in isolation from Sudan, Somalia, and Gulf power competition
