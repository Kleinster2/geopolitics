# Bill Bishop

Bill Bishop is the founder and editor of Sinocism, a China-focused newsletter that combines analysis, commentary, occasional original reporting, and curated Chinese- and English-language source links. His value for the geopolitics vault is source selection: surfacing how Chinese official media, ministries, and elite-policy channels are framing events before that framing becomes a broader Western narrative.
