# Manoj Kewalramani

## Overview
China analyst at the Takshashila Institution and author of *Smokeless War: China's Quest for Geopolitical Dominance*. He runs Tracking People's Daily and hosts *The Great Power Show*, which focuses on China, great-power competition, and the emerging world order.

## Relevance
Kewalramani is useful for the vault because he bridges Chinese source-reading, Indian strategic context, and great-power competition. His May 2026 interview with [[Howard Wang]] supplied the transcript basis for the [[Chinese total war doctrine]] note.

## May 2026 source-reading

Kewalramani's May 27 2026 Tracking People's Daily note on [[Gao Fei]]'s People's Daily article is useful because it separates the official Chinese text from the interpretive signal around it. His read is that Beijing's post-summit phrase [[Constructive strategic stability]] marks a discourse shift: Washington talked about guardrails in 2022-2023, while Beijing is now the side emphasizing the need to manage competition boundaries.

He also launched a companion project, "China's Vision for World Order," to map Chinese global initiatives and the Community with a Shared Future concept. That project fits this vault because the 2026 US-China stability formula is one part of the larger Chinese order vocabulary rather than an isolated bilateral phrase.
