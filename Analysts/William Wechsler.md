# William Wechsler

Senior Director of Middle East Programs at the [[Atlantic Council]]. Former senior Pentagon official (Deputy Assistant Secretary of Defense for Special Operations and Combating Terrorism).

## Key assessments

### 2026 Iran conflict — Gulf escalation ladder

On Gulf states allowing US missile launches from their territory as a step short of direct military engagement:

> "No country can indefinitely take attacks without any response. Over time, the Gulf countries will be under more pressure to respond militarily. But before they get to that point, they can allow the U.S. to use their territory."

The assessment frames territorial access as a middle rung on an escalation ladder: between pure self-defense (intercepting incoming) and direct offensive action. Each Iranian strike on Gulf states pushes them up the ladder.

*Source: WSJ (March 18, 2026)*
