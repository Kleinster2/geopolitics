# Farzin Nadimi

## Overview

Senior Fellow at the Washington Institute for Near East Policy specializing in Iran's military capabilities, regional security strategy, and defense-industrial networks. A primary authority on Iran's naval order of battle, including the IRGC Navy vs. regular Iranian Navy (IRIN) distinction, mine warfare capabilities, and submarine fleet.

## 2026 Iran Conflict — Mine Warfare Assessment

Assessed that Iran has "almost certainly" already deployed seabed mine devices in the [[Strait of Hormuz]] prior to or during the [[2026 Iran conflict]]: "They are waiting to be activated." Activation would use cables attached to shore installations or acoustic signals (FT, March 2026).

Iran's mine stockpile exceeds 6,000 devices, including modern designs capable of detonating in response to specific acoustic, magnetic, and pressure signatures (FT, March 2026).
