# Thomas Karako

Senior Fellow at the Center for Strategic and International Studies (CSIS), directing the Missile Defense Project. Focus on missile defense, air defense, precision strike, and defense acquisition.

## Key assessments

### 2026 Iran conflict — multi-domain integration and operational testing

On the decision to use PrSM and ATACMS land-based missiles from the opening hours:

> The decision reflected the Pentagon's desire to use Army ground-based missiles in conjunction with Air Force and Navy warplanes as well as longer-range cruise missiles to attack Iran.

Also characterized the PrSM deployment as "a bit of an operational test" — validating the weapon's combat performance, given the Pentagon had accelerated its fielding by delaying some testing (2025 GAO report).

*Source: WSJ (March 18, 2026)*
