# Mark Cancian

Senior Adviser at the Center for Strategic and International Studies (CSIS), International Security Program. Retired US Marine Corps colonel. Specializes in Pentagon budget, force structure, and munitions sustainability.

## Key assessments

### 2026 Iran conflict — inventory race

Framed the conflict's central military question as a contest of depletion:

> "It's a race to see will our inventories get low before the Iranian missile inventories get low."

US officials insist Iran cannot win this contest, but the daily cost of intercepting cheap Iranian drones and missiles with multi-million-dollar interceptors challenges that confidence.

*Source: Bloomberg (March 10-11, 2026)*
