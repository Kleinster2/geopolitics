# Kelly Grieco

Senior Fellow at the Stimson Center. Focus on US military alliances, force posture, and defense strategy. Previously at RAND.

## Key assessments

### 2026 Iran conflict — adversary precision strike revolution

On the unprecedented challenge Iran's arsenal posed to US forces:

> "The United States led the long-range precision strike revolution, and this is the first war where we're seeing the adversary have that kind of capability. It's putting stress on the system that we haven't seen before."

The assessment captures a structural shift: the US monopoly on precision strike has ended. Iran's combination of ballistic missiles and mass-produced drones forced the US into a defensive posture that consumed interceptors at unsustainable rates.

*Source: Bloomberg (March 10-11, 2026)*
