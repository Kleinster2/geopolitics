# Frank McKenzie

Retired US Marine Corps general. Former commander of US Central Command (CENTCOM), the combatant command responsible for military operations across the Middle East. Now a commentator on defense and Middle East security.

## Key assessments

### 2026 Iran conflict — land-based missile integration

On the use of PrSM and ATACMS from HIMARS launchers in the opening hours of Operation Epic Fury:

> "This has been planned for a long time. You are optimizing your weapons."

The comment indicates the multi-domain approach — integrating Army land-based missiles with Air Force and Navy systems to attack from multiple axes — was a deliberate pre-war planning feature, not an improvisation.

*Source: WSJ (March 18, 2026)*
