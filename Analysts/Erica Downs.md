# Erica Downs

Senior research scholar at the [[Columbia Center on Global Energy Policy]] (Columbia University SIPA). Research focuses on the geopolitics of energy, with particular expertise in China's energy relationships with [[Russia]], [[Venezuela]], and [[Iran]].

## Key contributions

- Expert on [[Chinese teapot refineries]] — the small independent refineries that buy sanctioned crude from Iran, Russia, and Venezuela at discount, operating outside the US dollar financial system
- Analysis of China's strategic petroleum reserves (~120 days of net crude oil import cover at 2025 levels, strategic + commercial stockpiles combined)
- "Shale evolution, not revolution" framing for China's unconventional gas development
- China as green tech superpower thesis: countries swapping energy import bills for Chinese renewable technology
- Historical context: 1960s Sino-Soviet energy cutoff as formative driver of China's energy security architecture
