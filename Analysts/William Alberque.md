# William Alberque

Europe-based Senior Fellow at the Pacific Forum. Focus on arms control, missile defense, and European security. Previously served at NATO's Weapons of Mass Destruction Non-Proliferation Centre.

## Key assessments

### 2026 Iran conflict — THAAD radar loss and global implications

On the first combat loss of a THAAD battery radar (damaged by Iranian strike in Jordan):

> "It's very expensive, it's very high end. I'm less concerned about that in terms of overall regional capabilities, but in terms of global capabilities that's a huge blow to us."

The distinction is critical: the US has only 8 THAAD systems deployed globally. Losing or degrading one to the Iran campaign weakens missile defense posture elsewhere — particularly in the Pacific against [[North Korea]] and [[China]].

*Source: Bloomberg (March 10-11, 2026)*
