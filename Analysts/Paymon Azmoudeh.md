# Paymon Azmoudeh

## Overview

Paris-based analyst and senior consultant at [[Forward Global]], focused on the geopolitical, socioeconomic, regulatory, governance, and civil-society dimensions of the [[Middle East]] and [[Africa]]. Forward Global describes Azmoudeh as a senior consultant since 2016 covering Middle East and Africa issues for client risk management, compliance, international expansion, and investment projects.

Azmoudeh is useful for the vault as a practitioner-analyst: his public commentary sits between regional political analysis and private-sector risk/compliance work, especially on [[Iran]], protest politics, sanctions/regulatory exposure, and conflict spillovers.
