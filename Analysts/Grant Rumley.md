# Grant Rumley

Senior Fellow at the Washington Institute for Near East Policy. Former Pentagon official. Focus on Middle East security policy and US defense.

## Key assessments

### 2026 Iran conflict — Pacific signaling through Iran

On the use of PrSM/ATACMS against Iranian naval vessels — the same missile types planned for deployment to Philippines and Pacific islands to counter [[China]]:

> Use of the missiles against Iran "will give Chinese military planners another wrinkle to consider."

The assessment captures the dual-use logic: Iran serves as a live-fire demonstration of the land-based anti-ship capability the US is building to defend [[Taiwan]] and the [[First Island Chain]].

*Source: WSJ (March 18, 2026)*
