# Steven Wills

## Overview

Former US Navy officer (20 years; served as executive officer of a mine countermeasures ship), now "Navalist" at the Center for Maritime Strategy (a program within the Navy League of the United States). Holds a PhD in Military History from Ohio University. Specializes in mine warfare operations and naval mine countermeasures.

## 2026 Iran Conflict — Mine Countermeasures Assessment

Assessed that US mine-clearing capabilities in the [[Strait of Hormuz]] during the [[2026 Iran conflict]] will be "a slow and laborious process." The replacement for the retired Avenger-class minesweepers — three littoral combat ships using helicopter-mounted lasers and unmanned sonar vehicles — works mine-by-mine rather than sweeping areas (FT, March 2026).

On detection challenges in the Gulf: dead sheep discarded from livestock ships, whose legs protrude when they bloat, can be mistaken for mines by lookouts — illustrating how difficult surface mine identification is even without sophisticated countermeasures.
